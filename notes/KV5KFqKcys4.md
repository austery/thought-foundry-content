**Michael Sullivan**: 当你站在超市货架前时，最核心的一点是寻找印有**特级初榨（Extra Virgin）**字样的橄榄油。这代表了橄榄油最高等级的品质标准。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Rosie Guerin**: “特级初榨”到底意味着什么？

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Rosie Guerin**: What does extra virgin mean?

</details>

**Michael Sullivan**: 简而言之，**特级初榨橄榄油（EVOO）**是通过纯机械压榨提取的橄榄果汁，在提取过程中完全没有使用任何化学溶剂或高温精炼手段。而且它的**游离脂肪酸（Free Fatty Acids）**含量极低。此外，它还必须通过由专业品鉴师组成的品鉴面板（Tasting Panel）的感官评审，确保没有任何缺陷风味。这就是特级初榨的真正含义。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Michael Sullivan**: Extra virgin olive oil means that it's unrefined. There are no chemicals involved in the process. It's low in free fatty acids, and it's also gone through a panel of tasters to make sure it doesn't have any sensory defects. So, that is what extra virgin means.

</details>

**Christine Cyr**: 那货架上那些写着“Pure（纯正）”或者“Light（轻质）”的橄榄油又是怎么回事呢？

<summary>Original English</summary>

**Christine Cyr**: What about the oils that say "pure" or "light"?

</details>

**Michael Sullivan**: 那些标注“纯正橄榄油”或“轻质橄榄油”的产品，实际上是经过化学精炼的橄榄油与少量初榨橄榄油的混合物。“Light”指的是它的风味和颜色较轻淡，而不是指热量较低！那些经过精炼的油失去了橄榄原本的风味、多酚和抗氧化物质。所以，如果你想要正宗、浓郁且健康的橄榄果香，一定要选择**特级初榨橄榄油**。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Michael Sullivan**: Pure and light olive oils are actually refined oils. "Light" refers to the flavor and color, not the calories! Refined oils have been processed with chemicals and heat to strip away defects, but that also strips away flavor and antioxidants. You want Extra Virgin.

</details>

**Rosie Guerin**: 标签上还有什么信息是非常关键的？

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Rosie Guerin**: What else should we be looking for on the label?

</details>

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>
