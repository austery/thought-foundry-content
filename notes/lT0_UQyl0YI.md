### 责任的经济学：AI无法替代的核心

核心问题不在于人工智能在技术上能否做到，而在于其责任范围。当专业人士每小时收费500美元时，客户支付的不是时间。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The core issue isn't whether AI can technically do it. It's in the liability surface area. When professionals charge $500 per hour, clients aren't paying for the time.</p>
</details>

他们支付的是在事情出错时，有人能承担法律和财务责任。这种责任空白造成了多重经济压力，摧毁了旨在取代专业人士的人工智能商业模式。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">They're paying for someone to carry legal and financial responsibility when things go wrong. This responsibility gap creates multiple economic pressures that destroy AI business models that are aimed at professional replacement.</p>
</details>

从你进入谈判阶段开始，你所支付的就不是他们工作的时间。当然，他们可以按可计费小时收费，但律师如何定价取决于他们自己。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">From the point when you enter the phase of negotiations and onward, what you're paying for is not an hour of their time. Sure, they can charge you for billable hours, but it's up to the lawyer on how they price their work.</p>
</details>

可以按小时计费，可以按固定费率，也可以是风险代理费。但你支付的是律师将承担的责任以及他们承诺交付的最终结果。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">It can be by the hour, it can be by a fixed rate or it can be a contingency fee. But what you are paying for is the responsibility that that lawyer is going to carry and the ultimate outcome that they promise to deliver.</p>
</details>

当人工智能创始人着手取代昂贵的专业人士时，即使是针对狭窄的用例，例如回到我们的例子，当一位创始人说：“我们想自动化专门处理离婚案件的家庭律师的工作。”这已经很狭窄了。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">When AI founders embark on a journey to replace an expensive professionals even for a narrow use case. So going back to our example, when a founder says, "We want to automate the work of a family lawyer who specializes in divorce cases." This is already narrow.</p>
</details>

不是所有律师，而是专门的家庭律师，而且在家庭律师中，只是那些专门处理离婚案件的。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Not all lawyers out there but specifically family lawyers and the within the family lawyers just the ones who specialize in divorce cases.</p>
</details>

但当他们这样做时，他们就开始玩弄风险转移机制，这暴露了专业人士替代的谬误。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">But when they do that they start playing with the riskshifting mechanism that exposes the professional replacement fallacy.</p>
</details>

当涉及子女、共同财产或配偶赡养费问题时，律师必须预见并规划各种结果，并在给定限制条件下计算每一步，最重要的是，他们必须在具有约束力的协议或法庭文件中以**记录律师**（Council of Record: 负责记录和代表客户的律师）的身份签字。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">When children joint property a spousal support are at issue. The lawyer must anticipate and plan for all kinds of outcomes and calculate each step given the constraints and most importantly sign their name as council of record on binding agreements or court filings.</p>
</details>

如果子女的监护安排不符合他们的最佳利益，律师将面临调查、纪律听证会，甚至可能被停职或吊销执照。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">If the child's custody arrangement does not serve their best interests, lawyers face investigations, disciplinary hearings, and possible suspension or even disbarment.</p>
</details>

仅为应对这些监管和律师协会的行动，法律费用就可能在5万到20万美元以上，还不包括任何判给客户的赔偿。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Defending against these regulatory and bar actions can cost between 50 to 200 plus,000 in legal fees alone, not counting any client compensation awarded.</p>
</details>

如果一份措辞不当的赡养令导致客户长期财务受损，律师的保险公司通常会支付赔偿。安大略省最低要求的责任保险范围是100万美元，许多律所的保额在200万到600万美元之间。

<details>

</details>

相比之下，人工智能平台通常将供应商责任上限设定为年度合同价值，通常每个客户或每项业务仅在1万到10万美元之间。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

</details>

大多数法律科技领域的人工智能产品规定，如果其软件遗漏资产或错误计算支持，退款仅限于订阅费，不覆盖实际损失。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

</details>

人工智能工具不为客户提供**职业责任保险**（Malpractice Insurance: 针对专业人士因疏忽或错误造成的损害提供赔偿的保险）。如果一个错误使客户损失50万美元，客户将承担风险和成本。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">AI tools do not provide malpractice insurance for clients. If an error costs the client $500,000, the client bears the risk and cost.</p>
</details>

没有百万美元的保险覆盖，没有保险保证，也没有针对人工智能供应商的监管追索权。
