### 塞勒姆小教堂与哈里特·塔布曼的历史联系

**主持人**: 我正站在一个有着悠久历史的小建筑前。这是安大略省圣凯瑟琳市的塞勒姆小教堂。许多人认为这是安大略历史最悠久的黑人教堂之一，始建于 1855 年。尽管它的建筑历史并不久远，但其会众的历史可以追溯到更早的时期。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>
**Host**: I'm standing in front of a small building with a big history. This is Salem Chapel in St. Catharines, Ontario. Many people believe this church is the oldest black church in Ontario. It was built in 1855, but its congregation dates back to 1814.
</details>

**主持人**: 最著名的一位成员是哈里特·塔布曼。她曾在 19世纪中叶居住在这一地区，并通过地下铁路组织了营救任务，许多被营救者最终定居于此地并成为教会员。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>
**Host**: Its most famous member, Harriet Tubman, who lived in the area during the mid-nineteenth century organized rescue missions via Underground Railroad conductors and many rescued by her ended up settling here becoming church members.
</details>

**罗切尔·布什**: 我是塞勒姆小教堂的信托人兼驻地历史学家，也是哈里特·塔布曼地下铁路国家历史遗址的负责人。同时我也是加拿大塔布曼旅游公司的所有者和运营商。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>
**Rochelle Bush**: My name is Rochelle Bush. I'm the trustee as well as resident historian of the Salem Chapel, British Methodist Episcopal Church Harriet Tubman Underground Railroad National Historic Site. I'm also the owner and operator of Tubman Tours Canada.
</details>
