### 传统球探的局限性与被忽视的天赋

现在，我想请大家想象一下体育界的伟大成就。你们看到了什么？很可能是勒布朗·詹姆斯、凯特琳·克拉克、萨克万·巴克利、西蒙·拜尔斯、C罗、梅西这样的运动员。

<p class="english-text">Now I'm going to start by getting you to visualize sporting greatness. And what do you see? It's probably athletes like LeBron James, Caitlin Clark, Saquon Barkley, Simone Biles, Ronaldo, Messi.</p>
</details>

你看，我们往往只关注来自少数几个国家的运动员。所以，如果你认为世界上最顶尖的人才都出自这些地方，我能理解。但这并非完全准确。因为天赋在他们取得体育成就之前就已经存在，而要实现其潜力，一路上需要经历许多事情。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">You see, we tend to think of athletes from quite a small subset of countries. So I'd forgive you for thinking that the best talent in the world just comes from those places. But that's not strictly true. Because talent existed long before their sporting greatness, and many things have to happen along the way for them to realize their potential.</p>
</details>

现在，一个常被忽视的事实是，他们获得了机会并被看见。你看，天赋无处不在。挑战在于如何发现这些天赋。那么，我们该怎么做呢？

<details>

<p class="english-text">Now, one thing that's commonly overlooked is that they got an opportunity and were visible. You see, talent exists everywhere. It's finding the talent that can be the challenge. So how do we do that?</p>

通常来说，这依靠的是**球探**（Scouting: 体育界发现和评估有潜力运动员的过程）。被球探发现是每个年轻运动员的梦想。你看到看台上那位老先生在做笔记，随后你接到电话，他想让你去试训。这对很多人来说都是一个激动人心的幻想。然而，球探的数量是有限的。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

我来自英国，那里的**切尔西足球俱乐部**（Chelsea Football Club: 一家位于伦敦的英格兰顶级足球俱乐部）拥有世界上最负盛名、资金最充裕的青训项目之一。然而，每支**英超**（Premier League: 英格兰足球超级联赛，英国顶级职业足球联赛）球队，或者说每位英超球探，每年只能考察大约2000名球员。但有数百万人参与这项运动。即使在这2000名球员中，也已经受到球员地理位置、成本因素和可及性因素的极大限制。

And even from those 2,000, it's already super limited by the player's geography, their cost factors, their access factors.</p>

现在，许多年轻人开始自己动手。他们将自己精彩表现的社交媒体短片上传到网上。但这样一来，我们只是用一个并非为人才识别而设计的算法，取代了人类来决定谁能被看到。我们该如何解决这个问题？切尔西足球俱乐部根本不可能看到世界上所有的天赋。或者说，这可能吗？

### AI赋能的体育人才发掘：aiScout的诞生

你看，现在有了**计算机视觉**（Computer Vision: 使计算机能够从图像或视频中获取、处理、分析和理解信息的技术）、**人工智能**（AI: Artificial Intelligence: 模拟人类智能的机器智能）和**深度学习**（Deep Learning: 机器学习的一个分支，通过多层神经网络学习数据表示）等技术，正在帮助我们弥合这一差距。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">You see, there's technologies now like computer vision, AI and deep learning, that are helping us bridge that gap.</p>
</details>

就我个人而言，我不是球探或教练。我的专业是**生物力学**（Biomechanics: 运动科学，研究生物体运动的力学原理）。像我这个行业的许多人一样，我们在体育实验室或俱乐部工作，职责是提高运动员表现或降低受伤风险。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Now, personally, I'm not a scout or a coach. My route was biomechanics, which is the science of motion. And like many people in my profession, we work in sports laboratories or with clubs with a remit of improving athlete performance or reducing injury risk.</p>
</details>

有一天，当我在实验室工作时，我现在的创始人兼首席执行官达伦·佩里斯带着他受伤的儿子里夫走了进来。我们开始用所有这些精密的设备分析里夫。我们聊起了球探问题，他指出球探工作是多么不公平和带有偏见。他亲身经历过。一个人的整个未来可能在一天之内，由一个有主观意见的人决定。他指出青少年球探工作是多么缺乏数据支持。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And I was working in my lab that one day, my now founder and CEO, Darren Peries, walked in with his son, Reef, who was struggling with an injury, and we started analyzing him with all this wonderful equipment. And we got talking about the scouting problem, and he noted just how unfair and biased scouting could be. He'd seen it himself. Entire futures could be decided in one day by one person who has an opinion. He noted how completely devoid of data youth scouting can be.</p>
</details>

他问我，如果我们能把所有这些实验室协议、所有数据、所有设备，都整合到一套标准化的智能手机训练中，这样世界上任何地方的任何孩子都能得到公平公正的测试，那会怎么样？对我来说，这是一个针对真实问题的绝妙愿景。

<details>

<p class="english-text">And he said to me, what if we took all this lab protocols, all the data, all the equipment, and put them into a set of standardized smartphone drills so any kid, anywhere in the world could be tested fairly and equitably?</p>
</details>

于是我加入了他的团队**ai.io**（ai.io: 一家利用人工智能技术为体育界提供解决方案的公司），我们在那里为所有体育项目构建基于AI的解决方案。我们构建的第一个产品就是**aiScout**（aiScout: ai.io公司开发的一款利用AI进行体育人才发掘的应用程序），用于解决当前的问题。

<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

</details>

### aiScout的工作原理与数据解读

简单来说，aiScout的工作原理是这样的：孩子们免费下载一个应用程序，然后直接通过手机录制自己完成预设训练的过程。我们利用云端的计算机视觉AI来分析这些视频。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And here's how it works, simply speaking. Kid downloads an app for free and they record themselves doing these predefined drills directly from the phone. We use computer-vision AI in the cloud to analyze that.</p>
</details>

我们分析22个关键的身体部位。我们甚至可以将2D视频转换为推断的3D模型，并将所有这些数据整合起来。我们能得到诸如他们跑步的方向、转弯方式、跳跃高度、速度、对称性、协调性等信息。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">We analyze 22 key body segments. We can even do things like turn the 2D video into an inferred 3D and bring all that together. We get things like what direction did they run, how did they turn, how high did they jump, the speed, the symmetry, the coordination, and so on.</p>
</details>

但收集这些原始数据只是问题的一部分。你必须知道如何解读这些数据，并且能够根据这些数据对运动员进行评分。最重要的是，你必须针对那些正在寻找人才的人，使数据具有特异性。特异性在这里是关键。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">But collecting that raw-level data is just one part of the problem. So you've got to know how to interpret that data, and you've got to be able to score an athlete based on that data. And most importantly, you've got to make it specific for those that are looking for talent. Specificity is key here.</p>
</details>

例如，在足球运动中——对于在场的美国观众来说就是“soccer”——每个球队、球探或教练根据他们当时的需求，寻找的东西都有所不同。有些球队或教练可能想要力量和速度兼备的运动员，而另一些则可能想要协调性、技术和出色身体动作的运动员。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">For example, in football -- soccer, for the Americans in the audience -- each team or scout or coach kind of look for a different thing depending on what they need at that given moment of time. So some teams or coaches might want athletes with power and pace, others might want coordination and technique and great body movement.</p>
</details>

因此，我们必须让aiScout根据不同的俱乐部进行定制。为了回答这些问题并真正开发出这款产品，我们与两支英超球队——**伯恩利足球俱乐部**（Burnley Football Club: 一家位于兰开夏郡伯恩利的英格兰职业足球俱乐部）和切尔西足球俱乐部——进行了合作。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">So we have to make our scout, we have to be tailored, on that club-by-club basis. Now to answer these questions and really to build out that product, we partner with two Premier League teams, Burnley Football Club and Chelsea Football Club.</p>
</details>

我们首先直接询问他们，如果我们能通过智能手机分析世界上任何一个孩子，并提供足球特有的指标，他们需要知道什么才能使其对他们有用并加以利用？有趣的是，他们说需要可比较、可基准化、可靠的数据。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And we started by just asking them directly, if we could analyze any kid in the world, from a smartphone and give you football-specific metrics, what do you need to know to make that relevant to you and to use it? And interestingly, they said, comparable, benchmarkable, reliable data.</p>
</details>

最重要的是，他们自己以及他们将要分析的孩子们，都需要理解数据来源于何处。不能有任何歧义。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And above all else, both themselves and the kids they'd be analyzing, would need to understand where the data is coming from. There can be no ambiguity.</p>
</details>

于是我们开始与他们一起开发这些预设的训练项目。它们包括十米冲刺、反向跳跃、绕锥运球、传球、射门。这些都不是新鲜事物。这些只是球探通常会用来评估球员的真实项目。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">So we started developing these predefined drills with them. They were ten-meter sprints, counter-movement jumps, they were dribbling through cones, they were passing, shooting. This is nothing new. This was just genuine things that scouts would normally look at to make an assessment of a player.</p>
</details>

然后我们与那些球探坐下来，查看了大量的视频。你们更喜欢球员A还是球员B？结果发现，球探的艺术实际上极其复杂。许多经验丰富的球探所做的事情都是非常直观的。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And then we sat down with those scouts and looked through a ton of video. Do you prefer player A or player B? Now it turns out the art of scouting is actually incredibly complex. So much of what experienced scout does is really intuitive.</p>
</details>

所以，尽管他们知道自己更喜欢球员B，而且我们大多数人看着这里的视频也能看出来，但他们无法向我们清楚地表达原因。因此，我们必须和他们坐在一起，提问，并将他们的见解转化为我们可以用来评分的东西。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">So whilst they knew they preferred player B, and maybe most of us can, looking at the video here, but they couldn't articulate it to us. So we had to sit with them, ask the questions, and turn their insights into something we could use to score.</p>
</details>

我们创建了算法。有了这个算法，我们处理了数千个视频。因此，我们开始建立跨年龄和性别的基准和标准。因为当然，你不能用分析13岁孩子的方式去分析22岁的人。22岁的人几乎总是更大、更强壮、更快。你需要将13岁的孩子与13岁的孩子进行比较，这样你才能真正看出谁是佼佼者。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">We created the algorithm. And with that algorithm, we just pumped thousands of videos through it. So we started to make benchmarks and standards across age and gender. Because of course, you can't analyze a 13-year-old to a 22-year-old. The 22-year-old is almost always going to be bigger, stronger, faster. You need to compare 13-year-olds to 13-year-olds so you can really see who stands out.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

<details>

Tens of thousands of kids now do this every single year. And the best ones, based on their data, get sent to an in-person talent ID day where the scouts make the decision on who gets those scholarships.</p>

这是一个很好的例子，说明我们是如何增强球探流程，而不仅仅是取代他们的流程。最成功的例子是，我们有很多这样的例子，其中一名球员通过共享社区电话下载了应用程序，他从未参加过有组织的体育运动，却为自己赢得了一份五年奖学金。

除此之外，就在去年，**国际奥委会**（IOC: International Olympic Committee: 负责组织现代奥林匹克运动会的国际非政府体育组织）及其当时的合作伙伴**英特尔**（Intel: 美国一家跨国半导体芯片制造商），就即将到来的塞内加尔青年奥运会与我们取得了联系。他们有点担心塞内加尔国家队没有足够的天赋来填补青年奥运会的所有参赛队伍。

### 技术的普适性与未来展望

这项技术的伟大之处在于：你想踢足球吗？你可以通过我们的应用程序为足球俱乐部试训。你也可以反过来做。根据你的优势，它可以告诉你可能擅长什么。
