### 权力人物的成功秘诀

如果你观察世界上最有权势的人，他们往往结合了两种特质：一种是卓越的**分析能力**（analytical ability），另一种是强烈的**竞争意识**（competitive mindset）。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">If you look at the most powerful people in the world, they combine two things, a skill set of being both really analytical and in the mindset of being really competitive.</p>
</details>

他们非常敢于承担风险，可以是特立独行者，坦率地说，有时也可能很难相处。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">They are very risk taking. They can be contrarian. They can be difficult, sometimes too, frankly.</p>
</details>

但他们真的、真的渴望在一个全球有八十亿人口、至少在西方大部分是**资本主义系统**（Capitalist System: 一种以生产资料私有制和市场经济为基础的经济制度）的体系中取得胜利。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">But they really, really want to win in a system where there are eight billion people in the world. It's at least in the West, a capitalist system for the most part.</p>
</details>

竞争心强的人能够脱颖而出，这并不令人震惊。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">No shock that competitive people kind of get ahead.</p>
</details>

如果你将这种竞争意识与一定程度的分析严谨性结合起来，这两者的结合将产生巨大的力量。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">If you combine that with having-- Some degree of analytic rigor those two things combined are are very powerful</p>
</details>
