### 明尼阿波利斯 ICE 枪击事件

**Nicholas Bogle**: 我是 Nicholas Bogle，是《纽约时报》的记者。我现在位于明尼阿波利斯 Portland A 街和 East 34 街的拐角处。现在大约是晚上 11:30，大约有 100 名抗议者仍然聚集在今天早些时候一名 ICE 探员枪杀 Renee Good 的十字路口。我的同事们正在明尼阿波利斯现场，那里一名 37 岁的女性，Renee Nicole Good，一位居住在该市的美国公民，被一名联邦官员枪杀，该官员是特朗普政府日益升级的移民镇压行动的一部分。多段在社交媒体上发布并经《纽约时报》核实的视频显示了枪击事件以及事件发生前后的情况。在视频中，这位女性的 SUV 停在街上，部分阻挡了一条车道。当车辆驶近时，她向他们挥手示意绕行，但一辆闪烁着灯光的卡车停下，联邦探员下车。他们告诉司机下车。一名探员试图打开驾驶员的车门并伸出手去。另一名探员穿过她 SUV 的前方，当她开始倒车时。然后她换入前进档并开始向前行驶。站在驾驶员侧前大灯附近的探员拔出手枪并开火。他继续射击，直到车辆从他身边驶过。对三个不同摄像头角度的分析表明，当他开火时，她正驾驶车辆远离探员，而且他似乎不在 SUV 的行驶路径上。在视频中，车辆随后加速并撞到停放的汽车和一根电线杆。明尼阿波利斯警察局长后来表示，受害者头部中弹，并在医院被宣告死亡。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Nicholas Bogle**: My name is Nicholas Bogle and I am a reporter for the New York Times. I am at the corner of Portland A and East 34th Street in Minneapolis. It's about 11:30 at night and there are about a 100 protesters still here at the intersection where an ICE agent fatally shot Renee Good earlier today. My colleagues are on the ground in Minneapolis where a 37year-old woman, Renee Nicole Good, a US citizen who lived in the city, was killed by a federal officer who was part of the Trump administration's escalating immigration crackdown. Multiple videos posted on social media and verified by the Times show the shooting and the moments before and after. In the footage, the woman's SUV is stopped in the street, partly blocking one lane. As cars approach, she waves at them to go around, but a truck flashing its lights stops and federal agents get out. They tell the driver to get out of her car. One agent tries to open the driver's door and reaches through the window. Another agent crosses in front of her SUV as she starts to back up. She then shifts into drive and starts to move forward. The agent standing at the front near the driver's side headlight pulls out a gun and fires. He continues shooting as the vehicle moves past him. A Times analysis of three different camera angles shows she was driving away from the officer when he opened fire and it appears he was not in the path of the SUV. In the footage, the vehicle then accelerates and crashes into parked cars and a utility pole. The Minneapolis police chief said later the victim had been shot in the head and was pronounced dead at a hospital.
