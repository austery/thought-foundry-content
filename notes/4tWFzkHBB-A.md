### 全球最强大的数据中心：Fairwater 2

这是**Fairwater 2**，目前全球最强大的数据中心。这座建筑中的**光网络技术**（Network Optics: 利用光纤传输数据的网络技术）容量，几乎相当于两年半前我们所有**Azure**（Microsoft Azure: 微软的云计算平台）数据中心的总和。它拥有约五百万个网络连接。这将有效地实现十倍的增长，相比于**GPD5**（一个大型语言模型）训练时所用的规模，这将是十倍的提升。

<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">This is Fairwater 2, the current most powerful data center in the world. Network optics in this building is almost as much as all of Azure across all our data centers two and a half years ago. It's got what, five million network connections. This would be effectively a 10x increase. 10x from what GPD5 was trained with.</p>
</details>

### 面向未来的大规模AI扩展

在一个区域内的不同站点之间以及两个区域之间，都拥有如此巨大的带宽。这是否意味着对未来扩展的一项重大押注？你们是否预计未来会出现需要跨越两个完全不同区域的巨型模型？我们的目标是能够将这些设施跨站点地整合起来。你们将在附近看到正在建设中的**Fairwater 4**，它也将接入**一拍比特网络**（Petabits Network: 每秒传输一千兆比特数据的超高速网络），以便我们能够以极高的速率将两者连接起来。之后，我们还将通过**智能广域网**（IWAN: Intelligent Wide Area Network，一种优化广域网连接的技术）连接到密尔沃基，那里有多个其他的Fairwater数据中心正在建设中。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">You've got all this bandwidth between different... sites in a region and between the two regions. So is this like a big bet on scaling in the future that you anticipate in the future? There's going to be some huge model that needs to require two whole different regions. The goal is to be able to put these things together across sites. Fairwater 4, which you're going to see under construction nearby, will also be on that one petabits network, so that we can actually link the two at a very high rate. And then basically we do the IWAN connecting to Milwaukee, where we have multiple other Fairwaters being built.</p>
