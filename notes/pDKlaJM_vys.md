### 军事行动的误区：为什么轰炸无法击败伊朗

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

You made some predictions then. Many of them have come true already and many of them still unfolding. But I wanted to get you back to talk about what the hell is going on. And I think that's kind of how I started last conversation. But there's so much that's being said and I get the sense that there's a truth that sits underneath there somewhere... My first question to you, professor, is who are you and who are you to speak on this subject matter?

</details>

**佩普教授**: 我是芝加哥大学的教授，在那教书快27年了。在此之前，我曾在美国空军任教，教授常规目标打击。我最初是想了解我们为什么会输掉越南战争，这促成了我后来的著作**《轰炸致胜》**（Bombing to Win）。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Professor Robert Pape**: I am a professor at the University of Chicago. I have been there for 26 years almost 27 years and before that I was a professor who taught for the US Air Force. I taught conventional targeting and I thought I was going to go into the foreign service. I wanted to understand how we lost the Vietnam War and this became the origins of Bombing to Win.

</details>

**史蒂芬**: 你所谓的“空中战役”具体指什么？

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**佩普教授**: “空中战役”是指军用飞机不仅是进行一天的单次袭击，而是持续数天、数周、数月，甚至像越南那样持续数年的行动。我研究的是，为什么有些国家在军事实力如此强大的情况下，进行了深思熟虑的空中战役，结果却依然是失败者。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Professor Robert Pape**: What I mean with an air campaign is when you have military aircraft who were not just doing a single raid bombing one target one day, but doing a campaign over days, weeks, months... Why is it that when a strong power really gets its act together, it's not careless, it's really thinking hard, it then applies this force, a campaign overtime and comes out a loser.

</details>

### 美国轰炸伊朗的模拟结果：无法触及的‘核心’

**史蒂芬**: 你花了21年时间模拟美国与伊朗之间的战争。在你的模型中，美国是如何走出这种局面的？

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Stephen**: And you modeled for 20 years a war with Iran versus the United States... how did America come out of this situation?

</details>

**佩普教授**: 调查结果非常一致：我们的轰炸机总能摧毁工业设施，但无论在哪一年模拟，我们都无法摧毁已经**浓缩的铀材料**。就像你在河里淘金，我们可以摧毁你的淘金盘，甚至摧毁整条河，但金子还在那里。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Professor Robert Pape**: There was a consistent set of findings you just couldn't ignore, Stephen, which is our bombers would always be able to destroy the target, the industrial facility that was enriching the uranium. The problem always was... you wouldn't be able to destroy the enriched material, the actual gold.

</details>

**史蒂芬**: 也就是说，轰炸只能把浓缩铀埋在废墟下，这只是在拖延时间，因为他们随时可以回来把它挖出来继续进程。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Stephen**: So they could bomb these sites where they're making the enriched uranium, but it wouldn't destroy the enriched uranium. It would just put it underneath a bunch of rubble... you're basically just kicking the can down the road.

**佩普教授**: 没错。而且他们可能会预判轰炸的到来并提前分散物资。去年底的“午夜之锤”行动（Operation Midnight Hammer），美国出动了 B2 轰炸机和巨型钻地弹（MOAB），一切都和我三周前给学生们演示的模型一模一样。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Professor Robert Pape**: That's right... And at the end of last year they did operation midnight hammer where they bombed the sides with these incredible... exactly as we did in class... almost exactly the platforms... every single thing we talked about unfolded just as we had modeled in class.

</details>

### 战争催生民族主义：伊朗反而更强大了

**佩普教授**: 我真正的专长是军事行动与**政治**的互动。你打击的不只是工业目标，这个国家的民众和政权会在政治上做出反应。我在研究越战时发现，民众的政治反应往往会压倒战术上的军事效果。轰炸反而会激发民众更努力地克服损失。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Professor Robert Pape**: My real specialty, Stephen, is the interaction of military action and politics. You're not just hitting an industrial target. The people in the country, the population, the regime, they're reacting to that politically... the political reactions by the population often are overwhelming the tactical military effects.

</details>

**佩普教授**: 现在的情况是，我们并没有削弱伊朗，反而**增强了伊朗**。伊朗已经意识到我们无法击败他们。我们可以炸毁他们地面上的发射器，但他们已经把无人机和导弹库深埋在地下。尽管美国释放了巨大的空中力量，技术领先，但我们无法阻止他们对霍尔木兹海峡船只的袭击。他们深知这一点，并正在极大地利用这一优势。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Professor Robert Pape**: We have strengthened Iran... Iran has figured out that we can't beat them... We can see anything that's above ground... But what they've been doing is they have been not just deeply burying their industrial enrichment facilities, they've been deeply burying their arsenals of drones, deeply burying their arsenals of missiles... we can't stop their drone attacks against the ships in the straight of Hormuz. They know it.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Professor Robert Pape**: We're at a branch, a fork in the road. There's no way to go back to February 27... either we go through with the ground war or Iran becomes an emerging not right away fourth center of world power.

</details>

**史蒂芬**: 请解释一下这个“权力中心”的概念。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Stephen**: Explain that to me.

**佩普教授**: 伊朗正在通过选择性封锁霍尔木兹海峡来产生政治权力，迫使其他国家屈服。亚洲80%到90%的石油经过那里。**印度**现在没有站在美国这边，而是保持中立甚至向伊朗靠拢。**日本**也在疏远美国。伊朗想要的正是让美国的亚洲盟友重新定向。

**佩普教授**: 此外，特朗普正在要求盟友自掏腰包加强防御，这实际上是拆掉了美军作为“安全保证人”的地位。在波斯湾，中东国家正在分裂成三个阵营：伊拉克在疏远我们；阿曼和卡塔尔在观望；沙特和阿联酋则开始寻求**巴基斯坦**作为安全保障。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

### 能源武器化：俄伊中的协作

**佩普教授**: 一旦我们撤退，伊朗的权力会迅速增长。它与**俄罗斯和中国**的关系将更加紧密。俄罗斯几乎立即向伊朗提供了瞄准美国军舰的各种信息。如果伊朗（拥有20%世界石油储备）和俄罗斯（11%）进行协作，将30%的石油撤出全球市场，让中国吸收，这将给美国和欧洲带来巨大的经济灾难。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Professor Robert Pape**: Russia almost immediately offered Iran military targeting information to target US ships... Iran has control now of 20% of the world's oil. Russia has 11%... That means there can be either formal or tacic cooperation to take 30% of the world's oil off the global market. Let China soak up a whole lot of that. And that can truly produce mega economic consequences for America, for Europe.

President Trump is bonding them together like never before.
