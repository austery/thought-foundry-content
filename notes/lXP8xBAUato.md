### 资本主义与“被债务缠身的人”

<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The market is no longer just about producing goods. The system is also maintained by producing "indebted man".</p>
</details>

</details>

<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
