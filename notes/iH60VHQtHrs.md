### 人类思维与进化过程的差异

人类的思维方式有点像工程师。我们首先设想一个期望的结果，然后思考如何以最短的路径实现它。我们可能会尝试一些试错，但会尽量将错误降到最低。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Humans sort of think like engineers. We think of what we want as an outcome and how we can get there sort of in the shortest distance. And we may use some trial and error, but we really try to minimize the error.</p>
</details>

然而，进化的过程恰恰相反，它始于错误。它从随机的变化开始，然后对这些变化进行尝试。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Whereas really these evolutionary processes start with error. They start with a random change and then try those things out.</p>
</details>

我们很难理解，世界上所有这些多样性和看似有序的现象，是如何从一个依赖于偶然性的过程中产生的。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

</details>

### 进化的阶梯：突变与选择

所以，**进化**（Evolution: 生物种群的遗传特征在世代间发生变化的过程）的真正含义是随时间推移而发生的变化。我们想知道这种变化是如何随时间发生的。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">So evolution, what it really means is change over time. So we wanna know how that change occurs over time.</p>
</details>

这个过程有两个维度，它有点像一个阶梯。其中一个过程是**突变**（Mutation: DNA序列中发生的随机改变），它就像阶梯的“上升”部分。这些突变是偶然发生的。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And there's two dimensions to this process and it kind of works like a staircase. And one process is mutation and that's the rise in the staircase. Those occur by chance.</p>
</details>

如果没有突变发生，所有事物都将是完全相同的。因此，你需要突变才能使个体彼此不同。这些突变是基因变化，是DNA中的变化。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">If mutation didn't happen, all things would be identical. So you need mutation to make individuals different from one another. Those mutations are genetic changes, changes in their DNA.</p>
</details>

如果偶然地，这种变化带来了有利于个体繁殖和生存的特性，它就会传播开来，这就是**自然选择**（Natural Selection: 生物在生存竞争中适者生存、不适者淘汰的过程），它就像阶梯的“水平延伸”部分。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">If by chance, that changes the property that favors reproduction, survival of the individuals, it will spread, and that's the selection process and that's sort of the run in the staircase.</p>
</details>

偶然性创造了发明，而自然选择则传播了这种偶然的发明。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Chance invents and natural selection propagates that chance invention.</p>
</details>

一旦这个过程发生，新的突变就可以在此基础上叠加，甚至可能进一步改变其特性，这又是阶梯的另一次“上升”。如果这种变化效果更好，它就会传播。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And once that process has happened, new mutations can then be added on top of that, maybe even changing the character a little bit further, and that's another rise in the staircase. And if that's working better, that's gonna spread.</p>
</details>

因此，正是突变的累积和选择的累积过程，将我们带上了那个阶梯。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And so it's the cumulative set of mutations and the cumulative process of selection that takes us up that staircase.</p>
</details>
