### 自动驾驶分级与市场策略

以防你不熟悉这个分级系统。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Just in case you're unfamiliar with this whole leveling thing.</p>
</details>

**零级自动化**（Level Zero）意味着没有自动化，你驾驶汽车，你负责一切。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Level zero is no automation. You drive the car. You drive everything. You're responsible for everything.</p>
</details>

**一级自动化**（Level One）是驾驶辅助，汽车可以自动完成一件事，比如**巡航控制**（Cruise control）、车道保持或制动，但你仍然控制其他一切。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Level one is driver assistance. The car can do one thing automatically. Cruise control, lane keeping, or braking, but you still control everything else.</p>
</details>

现在，**二级自动化**（Level Two）是部分自动化，汽车可以做两件事：转向和制动，或者转向和加速。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Now, level two, partial automation. The car can do two things. Steer and brake or steer and accelerate.</p>
</details>

因此，它可以在理想条件下在高速公路上自动驾驶，但你必须保持警惕，并能够随时接管。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">So, it can drive itself on a highway in ideal conditions, but you must stay alert and be able to take over at any point.</p>
</details>

你负责监督，你是责任人。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">You are supervising and you're the one responsible.</p>
</details>

**三级自动化**（Level Three）是有条件自动化，汽车可以在特定条件（如高速公路）下处理大多数驾驶任务，但如果出现问题或条件变化，它可能会要求你接管。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Level three, conditional automation. When the car can handle most driving tasks in specific conditions like highways, but it can demand you take control if something goes wrong or conditions change.</p>
</details>

在这种情况下，你负有部分责任，并处于待命状态。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">In this case, you're partially responsible and you're on standby.</p>
</details>

**四级自动化**（Level Four）是高度自动化，这就是**Cruise**所承诺的，也是**Waymo**最终能够实现的。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Level four, high automation. This is what Cruz promised and what Whimo was able to achieve in the end.</p>
</details>

汽车可以在没有人为干预的情况下自动驾驶。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</p>
<p class="english-text">The car can drive itself without human input.</p>
</details>

正常操作不需要人类驾驶员，但系统有局限性。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">A human driver is not needed for normal operation, but the system has limits.</p>
</details>

例如，只能在好天气或特定道路上。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">For example, only in good weather or only on certain roads.</p>
</details>

四级自动化意味着你可以在车里睡觉，不用注意。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Level four means that you can sleep in your car and not pay attention.</p>

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And finally, level five, full automation.</p>
</details>

汽车可以在任何地方、任何条件下自动驾驶。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The car can drive itself anywhere in all conditions.</p>
</details>

永远不需要人类，这是完全无人驾驶，目前尚不存在。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">No human needed ever. This is complete driverless and this does not exist yet.</p>
