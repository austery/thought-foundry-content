### 邮件问候与正文原则

让我们一起写一封邮件，从问候语开始。使用“Hi”加上名字通常就可以了。毕竟我们都是同事或朋友。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Let's write an email together, starting with the greeting. Hi, and a first name is probably fine. We're all friends here.</p>
</details>

将“Dear”和使用对方头衔的方式留给更正式的场合，例如官方简报或邀请函。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Save dear and using someone's title for more formal situations, such as an official briefing or an invitation.</p>
</details>

接下来是邮件正文。假设你沟通的每个人都比你聪明、比你更关心事务，也比你更忙碌。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The body of our email. Assume that everyone you're communicating with is smarter than you and cares more than you and is busier than you.</p>
</details>

避免使用**行话**（Jargon: 某一行业或群体内部使用的专业术语，外人难以理解），也不要寒暄。你不必每次发邮件都询问收件人的健康状况。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">No jargon, no small talk. You do not have to ask after your recipient's health every time you email them.</p>
</details>

### 邮件结尾与核心原则

邮件的结尾语也有正确的方式。最好的选择是“Best wishes”。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">There is a correct way to sign off an email. It is best wishes.</p>
</details>

“Best”和“All the best”也都可以接受。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Best and all the best are also acceptable.</p>
</details>

我的邮件礼仪哲学遵循一个基本原则：尽可能地减轻邮件带来的负担。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">My email etiquette philosophy is guided by one fundamental principle. Reducing the burden of email as much as possible.</p>
</details>
