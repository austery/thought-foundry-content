<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Bill**: I'm on the board of the Santa Fe Institute. The Santa Fe Institute studies complexity theory. I would describe complex systems as multivariable nonlinear systems. The weather, stock markets, all these things. There's consequences that can be first, second, third derivative. And you know, you you can't just think with a linear model or just think one variable because things can can go way off the path.
