### 毒液的致命性与作用机制

如果你不幸被咬，那会非常痛苦，因为它们的毒牙可以长达一厘米，比许多蛇的毒牙还要长。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">If you are unlucky enough to get bitten, it'll be painful, because their fangs can be up to one centimeter long, that's longer than many snake fangs.</p>
</details>

而且它们释放的毒液作用迅速且致命。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And the venom delivered is fast-acting and deadly.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

</details>

所以雄性蜘蛛的毒液实际上能生产出更高质量的**抗蛇毒血清**（Anti-venom: 用于中和毒液的药物）。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">So the males venom actually produces a higher quality anti-venom.</p>
</details>

我们见过成年人被雄性漏斗网蜘蛛咬伤后最快的死亡时间大约是76分钟，也就是一个多小时。

</details>

所以它是一种作用非常迅速的毒液。

<details>

<p class="english-text">So it's a very fast-acting, rapid venom.</p>
</details>

理想情况下，你需要保持冷静、静止，并包扎压迫固定绷带，然后尽快去医院。

<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

</details>

这种毒液含有一种蛋白质混合物，特别是**δ-六毒素**（Delta hexatoxin: 漏斗网蜘蛛毒液中的一种神经毒素）。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The venom contains a mixture of proteins, notably the delta hexatoxin.</p>
</details>

当它通过你的血液循环时，你会开始出汗、抽搐、流口水并起鸡皮疙瘩。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">As it travels through your bloodstream, you'll begin to sweat, twitch, salivate, and get goosebumps.</p>
</details>

这是因为毒液作用于神经细胞，即**神经元**（Neurons: 构成神经系统的基本单位）。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">This is because the venom acts on nerve cells, neurons.</p>
</details>

在正常情况下，**神经递质**（Neurotransmitters: 神经细胞之间传递信号的化学物质）会触发神经元打开**离子门**（Ion gates: 细胞膜上的通道），带正电荷的**钠离子**（Sodium ions: 一种重要的电解质）会涌入。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Under normal conditions, neurotransmitters trigger neurons to open ion gates, and positively-charged sodium ions flood in.</p>
</details>

从负电荷到正电荷的剧烈摆动会触发神经递质释放到下一个神经元，然后离子门关闭，细胞恢复到静息状态。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The dramatic swing from negative to positive triggers the release of neurotransmitters to the next neuron, and then the ion gates close, and the cell returns to its resting state.</p>
</details>

但漏斗网蜘蛛的毒液会阻止离子通道关闭。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">But funnel-web venom stops the ion channels from closing.</p>
</details>

因此，神经元会在整个神经系统中释放出大量的神经递质，比如肾上腺素。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">So the neuron unleashes a flood of neurotransmitters, like adrenaline, throughout the nervous system.</p>
</details>

所以通常受到精确控制的肌肉会接收到快速而混乱的信号，导致它们紧张或痉挛。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">So muscles, normally under precise control, receive rapid chaotic signals causing them to tense up or spasm.</p>
</details>

这对心脏来说是个大问题，心脏开始跳动得更快，血压升高，使毒液进一步扩散到全身。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Which is a big problem for the heart, which begins to pump faster, raising blood pressure, and spreading the venom further around the body.</p>
</details>

最终，你的身体会耗尽神经递质。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And eventually your body runs out of neurotransmitters.</p>
</details>

没有它们，神经细胞就无法再告诉肌肉该做什么，所以你实际上会瘫痪。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Without them, nerve cells can no longer tell muscles what to do, so you're effectively paralyzed.</p>
</details>

心脏或肺部衰竭只是时间问题。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And it's only a matter of time before your heart or your lungs give out.</p>
