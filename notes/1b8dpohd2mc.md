### 行政首脑论的胜利

**迈克尔·巴巴罗**: 没错，我们之前在节目中确实和同事查理·萨维奇讨论过多次“行政首脑论”——这个词确实有点拗口，得重复几遍才能说顺。简单来说，该理论的核心在于，为了保证行政分支的顺畅和高效运行，任何在行政系统内工作的官员都不应当被免除对总统的直接问责责任。这意味着，像联邦贸易委员会、**证券交易委员会**等独立机构负责人所享有的免受无故免职的职业保障，在这个逻辑下是站不住脚的。如果你坚信总统对整个行政分支拥有绝对和完整的统治权，那么国会设立的这些职位保护从根本上就是违宪的。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Michael Barbaro**: Right, we've talked about the unitary executive theory, I always have to say it several times, unitary executive theory, several times on the show with our colleague Charlie Savage, and basically the idea is that in a well-run executive branch, no one who works for the president should be protected from accountability from that president, and that these independent agencies like the Federal Trade Commission, like the Securities and Exchange Commission, and so on, that the job protections held by the leaders of these agencies don't make any sense. They're basically unconstitutional if you believe that the executive has true authority across his or her branch of government.

</details>
