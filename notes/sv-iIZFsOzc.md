### 战斗机代际划分与无人机协同

**Kelly Grieco:** 是的，这是一个很好的问题。我们经常谈论代际。我希望这些是具有非常明确标准的科学术语。但现实并非完全如此，人们甚至会对哪种飞机属于第五代还是第四代产生分歧。但基本上，我们试图通过代际术语将飞机分组，这些飞机在能力类型上似乎有某种飞跃或量级差异。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Yeah, so this is a great question. You know, we talk a lot about generations. I'd like to think that, you know, say that these are scientific terms that are used with these very clear criteria. The reality is that's not entirely the case and people will even disagree about what aircraft whether it belongs in you know fifth gen or fourth gen or but what basically what we try to do with the generational term is group aircraft together where it seems like there's some leap or or magnitude difference in terms of the kinds of capabilities it has.</p>
</details>

**Kelly Grieco:** 所以，如果你看主要在70年代开发的**第四代战机**（Fourth Generation Fighter: 强调机动性和近距离格斗能力的战斗机），重点是机动性和进行**空战**（Dogfight: 战斗机之间近距离的空中格斗）。而当你进入**第五代战机**（Fifth Generation Fighter: 具备隐身、超音速巡航、超机动性和信息融合能力的战斗机）时，我们看到了**隐身技术**（Stealth: 通过特殊设计和材料减少飞机被雷达、红外等探测到的技术）。这些飞机看起来更具未来感。武器舱是内置的，以帮助实现隐身。然后我们也看到了这些飞机开始真正演变为基于计算机的系统，带有传感器并试图整合这些传感器。这就是像F-35、F-22这样的飞机出现的地方。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">So if you look at fourth gen, which was developed mostly in the 70s, it was really the emphasis was on maneuverability and being able to dogfight. Whereas when you get to the fifth gen, this is when we see stealth. The aircraft that look much more futuristic. The weapon bays are internal to try to help with that stealth. And then we also see the start of really these turning into much more computer based systems with sensors and trying to integrate some of these sensors.
