### 损失厌恶：市场波动中的心理反应

我们对损失的感受强度大约是享受收益的两倍。当你思考这意味着什么时，就会发现当市场开始动摇、开始抛售时，我们全身都会感到不安。这让我们担心会损失金钱，损失投资。我们会想：“嘿，我们已经下跌了5%，最好做点什么，否则可能要跌10%了。”这种现象在心理学上被称为**损失厌恶**（Loss Aversion: 指人们面对同样数量的收益和损失时，认为损失带来的痛苦大于收益带来的快乐的心理现象）。

<p class="english-text">We feel losses just about twice as intensely as we enjoy gains. And when you think about what that means, when the markets begin to wobble, when they sell off, it sends our bodies aflame. It makes us concerned that we're going to lose our money, we're going to lose our investments. Hey, we're down 5%. We better do something or we're going to be down 10%.</p>

### 穿越风险：长期投资的关键

我们真正需要学习的是，市场每年都会有两到三次5%的下跌，而大约每三年中就有两年会出现10%的下跌。市场总是有涨有跌，风险与回报是同一枚硬币的两面。如果你想长期参与市场以获得回报，就必须经历风险、经历**回撤**（Drawdown: 资产净值从峰值跌落到谷值的幅度），经历那些困难时期。正是在这些时候，理解你的损失厌恶心理——即使你可能永远无法完全控制它——并阻止它影响你的决策，变得尤为重要。

<p class="english-text">What we really need to learn is that markets are down 5%, you know, two, three times a year. They're down 10%, just about two out of every three years. Markets go up and down. Risk and reward are two sides of the same coin. And if you want to be in it for the long term to garner the reward, you have to go through the risk, through the drawdown, through the difficult periods. And that's where getting your loss aversion, at least understanding it, you may never be able to get it under control, but understanding it and preventing it from affecting your decision making becomes really important.</p>
