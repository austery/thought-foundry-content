### AI应用中的教育鸿沟与实施挑战

我认为目前仍然存在巨大的教育鸿沟。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And I think there's still a big education gap.</p>
</details>

几周前流传的一项研究指出，95%的企业未能从**AI**（Artificial Intelligence: 人工智能）中获得价值。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">A study bandied around a couple of weeks ago where it said 95% of businesses get no value from AI.</p>
</details>

但我认为，这项研究最终表明问题并非出在模型本身，而是它们的**实施**（implementation: 将理论或计划付诸实践的过程）效果不佳。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">But I think there it turned out it was like not the models that were bad. It's like the implementations of them were not great.</p>
</details>

因此，我认为在如何更新对日常任务的看法、如何应用**模型**（AI models: 指人工智能算法和数据训练出的预测或生成工具）的能力，以及思考“我应该自己完成这项任务，还是可以将其交给模型来处理？”这些方面，存在着巨大的教育鸿沟。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">So I think there's just a big education gap for how you should update your view of your own day-to-day tasks and apply what capabilities models have and think about, like, hey, should I be doing this task myself or can I farm it out to a model?</p>
</details>

显然，有些公司在这方面做得很好，而另一些公司则对此一无所知，两者之间存在着明显的差距。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And there's definitely a delta of companies that really get this done well and others that are basically clueless.</p>
</details>
