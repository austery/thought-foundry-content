### 英国富人外流报告的争议

**亨利与合伙人**（Henley & Partners: 一家全球公民和居住咨询公司）每年都会发布其**私人财富移民报告**（Private Wealth Migration Report: 衡量全球高净值人士移民趋势的报告）。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Henley & Partners publish their Private Wealth Migration Report once a year.</p>
</details>

在他们最新一期的报告中，英国首次排名垫底，净流出16,500名百万富翁。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">In their latest edition, the UK ranks dead last — for the first time — with a net outflow of 16,500 millionaires.</p>
</details>

这个数字在议会中被引用，被报纸报道，并被用来构建关于英国全球竞争力的辩论。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">This figure has been quoted in Parliament, picked up by newspapers, and used to frame debates about Britain’s global competitiveness.</p>
</details>

这是一个非常令人担忧的数字，但也很难核实。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">It’s a very worrying number - but It’s also quite hard to verify.</p>
</details>

蒂姆·哈福德（Tim Harford）在他的BBC播客《或多或少》（More or Less）中调查了这一说法。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Tim Harford looked into this claim on his BBC podcast More or Less.</p>
</details>

他采访了新世界财富（New World Wealth）的研究主管，后者负责这项移民报告的基础研究，并解释说他们的估计依赖于社交媒体资料、新闻报道和公司备案。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">He interviewed the head of research at New World Wealth, who did the research underlying the migration report – who explained that their estimates rely on social media profiles, press coverage, and company filings.</p>
</details>

哈福德在他的播客中正确地指出，他们的样本不具代表性，不能从中得出结论。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Hartford argued in his podcast – quite rightly - that their sample was not representative and that you couldn’t draw conclusions from it…</p>
</details>

**税收政策协会**（Tax Policy Associates: 一家专注于税收政策研究的英国智库）的丹·奈德尔（Dan Neidle）更进一步，在他的网站上发表了一篇法证审查报告，指出该报告充斥着统计上的“危险信号”。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Dan Neidle – of Tax Policy Associates - went further - in a forensic review published on his website he pointed out that the report was riddled with statistical red flags.</p>
</details>

该公司在2023年至2025年间从其分析中剔除了所有房产财富，但其百万富翁数量几乎没有变化——奈德尔称，如果公布的方法论真实，这是一个“不可能”的结果。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The firm had dropped all property wealth from its analysis between 2023 and 2025, yet its millionaire counts barely moved — a result Neidle described as “impossible” if the published methodology were real.</p>
</details>

新世界财富后来告诉《金融时报》（FT），他们实际上并没有改变方法论，只是改变了对其的描述。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">New World Wealth later told the FT that they hadn’t actually changed their methodology – just their description of it.</p>
</details>

奈德尔在数据中发现了数字模式，其中偶数和以0或5结尾的数字频率异常高，而几乎没有1。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Neidle found digit patterns in the data with a suspiciously high frequency of even numbers and numbers where trailing digits cluster on 0s and 5s, with almost no 1s.</p>
</details>

他指出，从统计学上讲，这种自然发生的几率约为24万分之一。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Statistically – he points out - the chance of that occurring naturally is about 1 in 240,000.</p>
</details>

他表示，这进一步证明这些数字要么是手动创建的，要么是手动调整的。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">More evidence – he says - that the numbers were either manually created or manually adjusted.</p>
</details>

奈德尔的分析使用了成熟的技术来检测被操纵的数字。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Neidle’s analysis used well-established techniques to detect rigged numbers.</p>
</details>

他总结道，这些数字很可能是人为编造而非实际观察到的。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">He concluded – that the figures were likely engineered rather than observed.</p>
</details>

### 英国财富数据质量与非定居税制

这并不意味着富人没有离开英国，只是说很难知道他们是否真的离开了，因为缺乏高质量的数据。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Now this doesn’t mean that the rich are not leaving the UK – it just means that it is very difficult to know whether they are or not - simply because good data doesn’t exist.</p>
</details>

为了强调这种困难，英国**统计监管办公室**（UK Office for Statistics Regulation: 负责监督英国官方统计数据质量和完整性的机构）最近暂停了政府自己的**财富与资产调查**（Wealth and Assets Survey: 由国家统计局收集的关于家庭财富和资产的官方调查）的官方统计数据地位，此前**国家统计局**（Office for National Statistics, ONS: 英国负责收集和发布统计数据的政府部门）提出担忧，认为数据质量已不足以满足用户需求。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">To highlight the difficulty - the UK Office for Statistics Regulation recently suspended the official statistics status of the governments own Wealth and Assets Survey collected by the Office for National Statistics following concerns raised by the ONS that the data is no longer of sufficient quality to meet users’ needs.”</p>
</details>

《金融时报》在另一项分析中发现，自政府取消对**非定居居民**（non-domiciled residents: 指在英国居住但其永久住所不在英国的个人，享有特殊税收待遇）的优惠税收待遇并提高对富人的其他税费以来，大量公司董事已离开英国。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The Financial Times in a separate analysis - found that a wave of company directors have left Britain since the government abolished its favorable tax treatment for non-domiciled residents and raised other duties on the wealthy.</p>
</details>

他们分析了**公司注册处**（Companies House: 英国负责公司注册和备案的政府机构）的备案文件，寻找那些已将其地址更新为海外司法管辖区的英国公司董事。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">They analyzed Companies House filings, looking for directors of UK firms who’ve updated their addresses to overseas jurisdictions.</p>
</details>

《金融时报》在其专栏中强调，这也不是一个完美的方法，因为许多富人并不经营公司，而且许多董事也不会及时更新他们的备案。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">This is not perfect methodology either – which the FT highlighted in their column - as plenty of wealthy people don’t run companies, and plenty of directors don’t update their filings in a timely manner.</p>
</details>

但数据中可见一种趋势：在最近的时期，有3,790名董事报告离开了英国，高于前一年的2,712名，并且在4月份出现了一个高峰，这与新的税收变化相吻合。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">But a trend is visible in the data. In the most recent period, 3,790 directors reported leaving the UK, up from 2,712 the year before – and a spike that occurred in April coincides with the new tax changes.</p>
</details>

还有其他一些信号被报道。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">There are other signals being reported too.</p>
</details>

管家服务机构报告的安置数量减少。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Butler agencies report fewer placements.</p>
</details>

财富经理表示一些客户正在离开，伦敦的豪华房地产市场已经走软。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Wealth managers say some clients are leaving and London’s luxury property market has softened.</p>
</details>

这些都并非决定性证据，但也并非毫无意义。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">None of this is conclusive. But it’s not nothing either.</p>
</details>

英国的非定居税制并非近期发明。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The UK’s non-dom tax regime is not a recent invention.</p>
</details>

它可追溯到1799年由小威廉·皮特（William Pitt the Younger）为资助拿破仑战争而引入的第一部所得税法。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">It dates back to the very first income tax in 1799, introduced by William Pitt the Younger to fund the Napoleonic Wars.</p>
</details>

当时，所有居民仅对其在英国的收入征税。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">At the time, all residents were taxed only on UK income.</p>
</details>

1915年，规则发生变化：英国居民对其全球收入征税，除非他们可以声称非定居身份——这一豁免在一百多年里基本保持不变。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">In 1915, the rules changed: UK residents were taxed on worldwide income — unless they could claim non-domiciled status – and that carve-out remained largely intact for over a century.</p>
</details>

“非定居”指的是出于税收目的，其永久住所不在英国的英国居民。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">"Non-dom" describes a UK resident whose permanent home for tax purposes is outside the UK.</p>
</details>

根据英国税法，定居地一直有点特殊，它是他们认为的永久住所，也是他们联系最紧密的地方，它指的是他们的税收身份，不一定与他们的公民身份或居住身份相关——尽管这些因素可能会影响它。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Domicile under British tax law has always been a bit of a funny thing it’s the place they consider to be their permanent home and where they have the closest ties and it refers to their tax status, and doesn’t necessarily relate to their, citizenship or resident status - although it can be affected by these factors.</p>
</details>

### 非定居税制的工作原理与废除

非定居者只对其在英国赚取的收入缴纳英国税。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">A non-dom only paid UK tax on the money they earned in the UK.</p>
</details>

他们无需向英国政府缴纳在世界其他地方赚取的收入的税款，除非他们将这笔钱转回英国。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">They didn’t have to pay tax to the UK government on money made elsewhere in the world - unless they transfer that money back into the UK.</p>
</details>

对于富裕个人而言，这提供了一个显著且完全合法的节税机会，特别是如果你能将一个低税率国家作为你的定居地。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">For wealthy individuals, this presented the opportunity for significant - and entirely legal – tax savings – especially if you could claim a lower-tax country as your domicile.</p>
</details>

最著名的非定居者之一是前首相里希·苏纳克（Rishi Sunak）的妻子，她在几年前爆出丑闻后宣布将开始对其全球收入缴纳英国税。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">One of the best known non-doms was former Prime Minister Rishi Sunak's wife – who after a scandal broke a few years ago – announced that she would start paying UK tax on her worldwide income.</p>
</details>

多年来，改革非定居规则的尝试频繁发生。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Attempts to reform the non-dom rules over the years were frequent.</p>
</details>

工党在1974年差点废除了它。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Labour nearly abolished it in 1974.</p>
</details>

奈杰尔·劳森（Nigel Lawson）在1988年再次尝试。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Nigel Lawson tried again in 1988.</p>
</details>

在金融危机期间，该制度在2008年收紧，对长期非定居者引入了年度费用——如果你在过去九年中有七年居住在英国，每年必须支付3万英镑；如果你在过去十四年中有十二年居住在英国，每年必须支付6万英镑。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">During the financial crisis - the regime was tightened in 2008 – where annual charges were introduced for long-term non-doms — you had to pay £30,000 a year if you had been resident in the UK for seven of the past nine years, and £60,000 a year if you had been resident for twelve of the past fourteen years.</p>
</details>

**汇款制**（remittance basis: 一种税收制度，非定居者只需对其汇入英国的海外收入和收益征税）得以保留，但其获取变得更加昂贵和有条件。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The remittance basis was preserved, but access became more expensive and conditional.</p>
</details>

乔治·奥斯本（George Osborne）在2017年进行了进一步的修改，取消了那些在过去二十年中有十五年居住在英国的人的福利。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">George Osborne made further changes in 2017, removing benefits for those who had lived in the UK for fifteen of the past twenty years.</p>
</details>

但每次进行调整时，财政部官员都会警告税收外逃，而部长们则会退缩。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">But each time adjustments were made, Treasury officials warned of tax flight, and ministers backed down.</p>
</details>

人们担心，将富人赶到国外会比从他们海外收入征税中获得的收益对经济造成更大的损害。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The fear was that driving the wealthy abroad would harm the economy more than it would benefit from taxing them on their foreign earned income.</p>
</details>

2024年3月，时任财政大臣杰里米·亨特（Jeremy Hunt）在大选前宣布废除非定居规则。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">In March 2024, then-Chancellor Jeremy Hunt announced the abolition of the non dom rule ahead of the general elections.</p>
</details>

10月，工党财政大臣雷切尔·里夫斯（Rachel Reeves）确认了这项政策的终结，并将其扩展到离岸信托，以防止遗产税避税。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">In October, Labour Chancellor Rachel Reeves confirmed the policy’s end, extending it to offshore trusts to prevent inheritance tax avoidance.</p>
</details>

现在，一旦个人在英国居住满四年，其海外收入和收益将按赚取时在英国征税。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Now, foreign income and gains are taxed in the UK - as they are earned - once an individual has been resident in the UK for four years.</p>
</details>

此外，遗产税将在居住十年后适用于全球资产，并在离开后继续适用十年。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">On top of that, inheritance tax will apply to worldwide assets after ten years, and continue for ten years after departure.</p>
</details>

“被排除财产信托”的漏洞已被堵塞，出于税收目的的定居概念也已取消。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The excluded property trust loophole is closed and the concept of domicile for tax purposes has been retired.</p>
</details>

### 政策过渡期与全球税收竞争

目前存在过渡规则。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">There are transitional rules.</p>
</details>

2025年4月之前持有的资产可以重新估值。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Assets held before April 2025 can be rebased.</p>
</details>

海外收入和收益可以在有限时间内以12%的统一税率带入英国。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Foreign income and gains can be brought into the UK at a flat 12% rate for a limited time.</p>
</details>

新来者在“合格新居民”制度下享有四年的宽限期。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">New arrivals get a four-year grace period under the “Qualifying New Resident” regime.</p>
</details>

但总体方向是明确的：英国不再是全球流动财富的避税天堂。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">But the overall direction is clear: the UK is no longer a tax haven for the globally mobile.</p>
</details>

**预算责任办公室**（Office for Budget Responsibility: 英国独立的公共财政监督机构）表示，他们预计多达25%的非定居者将离开英国。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The Office for Budget Responsibility say that they expect up to 25% of non-doms to leave the UK.</p>
</details>

财政部预计五年内将带来127亿英镑的收入。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The Treasury forecasts £12.7 billion pounds in revenue over five years.</p>
</details>

华威大学（Warwick University）的研究表明，大规模离境的担忧可能被夸大了——因为当奥斯本2017年的改革生效时，只有6%受影响的非定居者离开了英国。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Research from Warwick University suggests the fears of mass departure may be overstated – as when Osborne’s 2017 change took effect, only 6% of affected non-doms left the UK.</p>
</details>

那些留下的人缴纳了显著更多的税款——平均每年额外10万英镑，预测中的大规模外流并未发生。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Those who stayed paid significantly more tax — on average, an extra £100,000 pounds per year and the predicted mass exodus never came.</p>
</details>

辩论尚未结束——今年6月，《金融时报》报道称，在伦敦金融城（City of London）的游说和一系列高调离境事件之后，财政大臣雷切尔·里夫斯正在探索部分逆转遗产税改革。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The debate is still not over – this June - the Financial Times reported that Chancellor Rachel Reeves was exploring a partial reversal of the inheritance tax changes, following lobbying from the City of London and a spate of high-profile departures.</p>
</details>

据说政府正在考虑调整十年追溯期或重新引入信托保护。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The government is said to be considering adjustments to the ten-year tail or reintroducing trust protections.</p>
</details>

目前尚未发布正式公告，但财政部正在密切关注其影响。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">No formal announcement has been made, but the Treasury is watching the fallout closely.</p>
</details>

有迹象表明一些富人正在离开——伦敦的豪华房地产市场已经走软，特别是在富裕外国人倾向居住的社区。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">There are signs that some of the rich are leaving - London’s luxury property market has softened – in particular in the neighborhoods where wealthy foreigners tend to live.</p>
</details>

管家服务机构报告的安置数量减少。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Butler agencies report fewer placements.</p>
</details>

财富经理表示一些客户正在离开。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Wealth managers say some clients are leaving.</p>
</details>

梅费尔（Mayfair）和贝尔格莱维亚（Belgravia）的招聘人员收到的超高净值人士咨询减少。
