### 后向整合与美元分红保证

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

So we do what you call a uh uh you know backward integration which means what we are importing now we produce it. You produce what we need and we are now producing things that when you wake up as a human being every morning you must use part of what we produce.

### 非洲炼油厂：挑战行业“黑手党”

**尼古拉·坦根**: 阿利科，回顾整个集团，你认为哪三个决策最关键，促成了现在的成功？

<details>

</details>

**阿利科·丹格特**: 重大的决定是进入水泥行业，试图拯救许多陷入困境的非洲国家。在赞比亚、刚果布、尼日利亚等国，水泥曾卖到 250 美元一吨，而且在尼日利亚你甚至要付钱后等上 3 个月。我们意识到，那些做水泥生意的人并没有认真想让水泥普及。所以我们进场并进行了重金投资。目前我们在 14 个国家拥有水泥业务。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Aliko Dangote**: Actually major decision is to now go into cement in trying to save a lot of African countries because they are struggling cement was selling for $250 a ton in countries like Zambia in Congo Brazzaville in Nigeria you have to pay and wait for 3 months So we realized that look people who are in cement they are not really serious about making cement available. So we now came in and we have now invested heavily. And you are in 14 countries in cement. We're in 14 countries.

</details>

**阿利科·丹格特**: 再来看看非洲的石油。我们有很多产油国，但它们不炼油。它们出口原油，然后进口成品油，进口成本非常高。这消耗了我们大量的储备。以尼日利亚为例，加油排队的问题已经持续了 52 年。有时候人们在圣诞节期间可能要排两天队才能买到油。对于一个产油国来说，这太不可思议了。政府炼油厂都废弃了，经营不善。所以我做了一个大胆的决定：我要建一个炼油厂。

<summary>Original English</summary>

So they export the crude oil and now they import the products and importation cost a lot of money. It actually goes deep into our own reserves. So when I look at Nigeria for example today I say okay fine but we've been having fuel queues for 52 years. Sometimes people used to stay at during Christmas you might have to go and queue for two days trying to buy fuel for a country that produces oil and we have tried government refineries they all destroyed they are not really doing well so we said okay fine let me take this bold decision.

</details>
