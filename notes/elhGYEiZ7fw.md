### 君主制还是共和制？

**Jason**: 两个简短的问题。在任何情况下，它会是君主制吗？毕竟你是皇太子，但在民主国家没有王子。第二，你理想中的转型角色是什么？你是否想竞选总统，领导国家走出这 47 年的噩梦？

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Jason**: Two quick questions. Under no circumstances, would that be a monarchy? Uh I think uh is a question everybody has for you because you're the prince. This is a democracy. We don't have princes in democracies. And then number two, what do you ideally see as your role in the transition? And then would you like to have the opportunity to run for president and to lead the country out of this horrific nightmare of 47 years?

</details>

**Reza Pahlavi**: 我的重点是**过程**，而非结果。作为一个**中立的仲裁者**，我不偏袒任何一种形式。世界上既有成功的共和制（如印度、法国、美国），也有成功的议会君主制（如日本、瑞典、西班牙）。最终由伊朗人民选择。我在这里是作为通往那个结果的**桥梁**。转型期领导人的职责不是竞选公职，也不是为了个人的权力和地位，而是团结国家走向共同目标，让国民决定他们喜欢的制度。**投票箱**是衡量民意的唯一手段。这次与 50 年前不同，当时没人知道霍梅尼提议的所谓“教法学家治国”到底是什么。而这一次，伊朗人民应该在完全透明的情况下，了解呈现在他们面前的不同模型。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Reza Pahlavi**: My focus is on the process, not the outcome. And in as a neutral arbiter, I'm not taking sides with one form versus the other. That's for the people of Iran to choose. And I think the world has seen successful models in both a republican system and a monarchic system. ... So at the end of the day, you know, again, uh it's not for me to choose the outcome. I'm here to be a bridge towards that outcome. That's the function of a transitional leader, not to run for office or not to have an aim for personal uh position or power or authority, but to be the element that unites the country towards a common purpose and allow the nation to decide what is their preferred system.
