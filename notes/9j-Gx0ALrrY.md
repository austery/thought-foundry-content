### 快速缓解压力的实用技巧

如果您今天感到压力，我有三个快速技巧可以帮助您度过这一天。

<p class="english-text">So if you are feeling stressed today, I have three quick tips to get you through your day.</p>

### 技巧一：近与远焦点法

第一个技巧是**近与远焦点法**。要进行这项练习，请伸出您的手指，指向天空，然后将其一直移到您的鼻尖并尝试聚焦。当您慢慢将手指移开时，即使它看起来像两个，您也要继续缓慢地将其从脸部移开，直到它变成一个。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The first step is the near and the far. In order to do this, go ahead and take your finger and pointing it up to the sky, bring it all the way to your nose and try to focus on it. As you slowly pull that finger away, even though it looks like two, you want to continue slowly moving that away from your face until it's one.</p>
</details>

然后，再次将手指移回鼻尖，它可能会变得有点摇晃，但这没关系，因为当我们尝试聚焦时，我们正在利用**辐辏与发散**（Convergence and Divergence: 眼睛在看近处和远处物体时，视线由发散到聚焦或由聚焦到发散的调节过程）的力量来帮助平静我们大脑中的恐惧中心——**杏仁核**（Amygdala: 大脑中处理情绪，特别是恐惧和焦虑的区域）。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">and then go ahead and bring that finger back towards your nose, and it's going to get kind of wibbly wobbly, but that's okay because as we are trying to focus on that, we are using the power of convergence and divergence to help calm the amygdala, that fear center in our brain.</p>
</details>

### 技巧二：捏谷法

接下来是**捏谷法**。通过捏谷法，我们将触及并激活我们的**迷走神经**（Vagus Nerve: 人体最长的脑神经，在调节心率、消化和压力反应中发挥关键作用），以帮助平静恐惧中心。我将用左手的拇指和食指做出一个“L”形，然后用右手的拇指和食指做出钳子状。接着，我将按摩左手拇指和食指之间那块肉多的地方。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Next one is pinch the valley. So in pinch the valley we are going to be accessing and activating our Vegas nerve to help calm down that fear center. I'm gonna make an L with my left hand with my thumb and forefinger I'm gonna take my right hand and make some pincers with my thumb and forefinger And I'm gonna just massage the in between meaty bit in between that thumb and forefinger.</p>
</details>

您不需要用力到留下淤青，但施加一些适当的压力即可，这是您可以随时随地进行的练习。完成一只手后，您可以换到另一只手。这是我最喜欢的技巧之一，因为我可以在桌子底下做，可以在开车时做，或者在排队时做。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Now you don't need to dig in and leave a bruise, but adding some good pressure and this is something that you can do anywhere anytime. Then once you're done on that hand you can switch it up to the other hand. This is one of my absolute favorites because I can do it under the table, I can do it while driving or standing in line somewhere.</p>
</details>

迷走神经是人体最长的脑神经，它在我们的神经系统的**战斗或逃跑反应**（Fight or Flight: 身体在面对感知到的威胁时产生的生理反应，为战斗或逃跑做准备）中发挥作用。因此，当迷走神经被激活时，它会向我们的大脑和身体发出信号，表明我们可以恢复平静，因为一切都是安全的。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And the vagus nerve, which is going to be the longest cranial nerve in the body, that is going to have a role in our fight or flight part of our nervous system. So when that vagus nerve is activated, that is signaling to our brain and our body that we can return to a sense of calm because all is safe.</p>
</details>

### 技巧三：轻叩勇气法

最后但同样重要的是**轻叩勇气法**。我将用两根食指，从眉毛之间开始，轻轻施压，从额头部位开始画心形，然后绕过眼睛，向下到颧骨，再到下巴。如果您觉得可以，还可以越过喉咙，轻叩到肩膀。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And last but not least is going to be tapping into courage. I'm going to take my two index fingers and just start right between the eyebrows and with some gentle pressure, I'm going to draw hearts starting at that forehead piece and moving around my eyes down under my cheekbones and to my chin. And then if you're feeling crazy, jumping over your throat and tapping your way towards your shoulders.</p>
</details>

您可以在需要额外勇气的时候多次进行这项练习，比如在会议前躲在洗手间里，或者在车里努力应对拥堵的交通时。所以，下次当您感到压力时，尝试其中一个技巧，然后回来在评论中告诉我们它是否对您有效。

<p class="english-text">Now you can do this many times as you are trying to get a little bit of extra courage before that meeting as you're hiding in the bathroom. Or maybe you're in the car trying to navigate through some stressful traffic. So the next time that you're feeling stressed, try one of these and come back, let us know in the comments if it worked for you.</p>
