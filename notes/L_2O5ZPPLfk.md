### AlphaGo的里程碑：第37步

在开发**AlphaGo**（一个由DeepMind公司开发的围棋人工智能程序）时，我们曾与当时世界上最优秀的围棋选手之一对弈。在五局比赛的第二局中，第37步出现了，AlphaGo下出了一步非常出人意料、非传统的棋，令许多职业围棋选手感到惊讶。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Move 37, when we were building AlphaGo, we were playing one of the best players in the world at the time. Move 37 happened during the second game of the five-game match, where AlphaGo played like a really unexpected, unconventional move that surprised many professional Go players.</p>
</details>

评论员称这一步“颇具创意，出人意料”。最终，AlphaGo赢得了那场比赛。这早期迹象表明，人工智能也能做出真正新颖和富有创造性的事情，而不仅仅是模仿其训练数据。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The commentator said it was, you know, pretty creative, unexpected. And then ultimately, AlphaGo ended up winning that game. An early sign that it can also do something that is truly novel and creative, that you might not expect just from imitating its training data.</p>
</details>

### AI创造力的本质与挑战

利用**语言模型**（Language Models: 一种基于大量文本数据训练，能够理解和生成人类语言的人工智能模型）来创造新颖的事物实际上非常容易。然而，真正的难点在于如何创造出既有用又有趣的新颖事物。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Creating novel things is actually very easy with language models. The hard part is creating novel things that are useful and interesting.</p>
</details>
