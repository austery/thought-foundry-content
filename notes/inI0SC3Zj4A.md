### “无限套利”机制与市场动态

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

由于许多投资者不擅长数学，**Strategy**公司发现他们可以以远高于其所持比特币价值的溢价出售股票，然后利用出售股票筹集的资金购买更多的比特币，从而推高比特币价格，也推高了他们的股价——这简直是一个“无限套利”的漏洞。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Because a lot of investors are not very good at math, strategy worked out that they could sell their stock at a large premium to the value of the bitcoins that they own and then use that money that they raised by selling stock to buy even more bitcoins, pushing the bitcoin price higher and their stock price higher, too. an infinite money glitch.</p>
</details>

如果有人试图向你讲解**有效市场假说**（efficient markets hypothesis: 认为金融市场价格充分反映了所有可用信息，因此投资者无法持续获得超额收益），请务必让他们向你解释这种动态。
