### AI、AGI与ASI的定义与悖论

在人工智能领域，通常认为有三个维度，分别是**AI**（Artificial Intelligence: 人工智能，广义概念）、AGI和**ASI**（Artificial Super Intelligence: 人工超级智能）。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">It is considered that there are three dimensions so to say in the realm of artificial intelligence AI, AGI and ASI.</p>
</details>
AI代表**人工狭义智能**（Artificial Narrow Intelligence: 专注于执行特定任务的AI）。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">AI stands for artificial narrow intelligence.</p>
</details>
它的目的是极其出色地执行单一特定任务，就像**Claude**（Anthropic公司开发的大型语言模型）生成文本，或者**Siri**（苹果公司开发的语音助手）在很大程度上识别语音，或者你的手机识别你的面部一样。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The purpose of the A and I is to perform a single specific task extremely well like the way Claude is generating text or Siri recognizing voice to a very certain extent or your phone recognizing your face.</p>
</details>
这是2025年目前唯一存在的AI类型。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">This is the only type of AI that currently exists in 2025.</p>
</details>
AGI代表人工通用智能。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">AGI stands for artificial general intelligence.</p>
</details>
AGI是一个理论术语，这里的关键词是“理论”。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">AGI is a theoretical term. The operative word here being theoretical.</p>
</details>
AGI据称能在学习、推理或知识迁移方面达到人类水平的智能，并且理想情况下，无需重新训练就能解决新问题。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">AGI would supposedly match human level intelligence in the ability to learn, reason or transfer knowledge and ideally solve novel problems without having to be retrained.</p>
</details>
你可能见过最接近AGI的例子是电影中类似人类的机器人。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The closest example of Agi that you might have seen is a human resembling robot in the movies.</p>
</details>
而ASI，即人工超级智能，则是一个纯粹的假设概念，指AI将在所有领域超越人类智能。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And ASI which stands for artificial super intelligence is a purely hypothetical concept of AI that would surpass human intelligence across all domains.</p>
</details>
ASI在理论上将能够比任何在世的人类更快地解决问题和学习，并具备自主改进自身的能力，这本质上就是**技术奇点**（Technological Singularity: 指人工智能超越人类智能，导致技术增长失控的假设未来）。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">ASI would hypothetically be able to solve problems and learn far faster than any human alive and have the ability to improve itself autonomously which is essentially technological singularity if I may.</p>
</details>
让我们回到这次对话的主题——AGI。

</details>
AGI的问题在于，很少有人能真正定义它意味着什么。
<details>

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">But ironically, there are quite a few people in the general public, so to say, who would gladly tell you the definition of AGI, thinking that they're well aware of what it is and what it's supposed to do.</p>
</details>
如果你搜索AGI的定义，你很可能会遇到“意识”这个词。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Now, if you search for the definition of AGI, the word that you're going to come across is going to be consciousness.</p>
