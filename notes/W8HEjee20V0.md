### 特斯拉销量暴跌与马斯克天价薪酬

今年7月，特斯拉在英国的销量不足一千辆。这并非笔误。这家曾定义电动汽车（EV: Electric Vehicle）革命的公司，其在英国的销量暴跌了近60%，而其中国竞争对手**比亚迪**（BYD: Build Your Dreams，一家中国汽车制造商）则销量飙升，增长了四倍，在英国以3比1的比例超越了特斯拉。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">This July, Tesla sold fewer than a thousand cars in the UK. That's not an error. The company that once defined the electric vehicle revolution saw its British sales collapse by nearly 60% while its Chinese rival BYD surged ahead, quadrupling its sales and outselling Tesla 3 to 1 in the UK.</p>
</details>

就在这条新闻爆出的同一周，特斯拉董事会授予埃隆·马斯克（Elon Musk）一份价值290亿美元的薪酬方案。让我们思考一下：销量下降，薪酬却上升。这是什么样的公司会做出的事情？

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">In the same week that this news broke, Tesla's board awarded Elon Musk a $29 billion pay package. Let's think about that for just one moment. Sales down, pay up. What kind of company does this?</p>
</details>

这不仅仅是一个糟糕的月份或一笔巨额薪酬的故事，它揭示了特斯拉扭曲的现实，在那里，常规规则不适用，基本开支和高管薪酬无论业绩如何，都在不断上涨。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">This isn't just a story of one bad month or one big payday. It's a look inside Tesla's warped reality where the usual rules don't apply. fundamental spend and executive pay floats ever upward regardless of results.</p>
</details>

支持者会辩称，马斯克最新的奖励部分是追溯性的，旨在弥补此前被特拉华州法院否决的560亿美元薪酬方案。然而，这份新薪酬方案的时机和规模引发了人们对特斯拉董事会如何定义成功的质疑。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Defenders will argue that Musk's latest award is partly retroactive, compensating him for a previous 56 billion package struck down by the Delaware courts. But the timing and the scale of this new payground raise questions about how Tesla's board defined success.</p>
</details>

随着竞争对手不断取得进展，诉讼堆积如山，高管纷纷离职，特斯拉的股价却出人意料地保持稳定。这种稳定性可能表明投资者信心，或者至少是漠不关心。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">As competitors gain ground, lawsuits pile up, and executives flee, Tesla's stock remains surprisingly stable. That stability might suggest investor confidence, or at least indifference.</p>
</details>

但如果你深入观察，会发现不满的迹象日益增多：股东诉讼、治理批评以及监管机构日益严格的审查。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">But if you look beneath the surface, there are growing signs of dissatisfaction, shareholder lawsuits, governance critiques, and growing scrutiny from regulators.</p>
</details>

让我们审视特斯拉当前核心的矛盾：**Cybertruck**（特斯拉电动皮卡车型）的惨败、董事会闹剧、**Robo-taxi**（自动驾驶出租车）的幻想，以及马斯克的雄心与特斯拉现实之间日益增长的紧张关系。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Let's examine the contradictions at the heart of Tesla's current moment. the Cybertruck debacle, the boardroom drama, the robo taxi fantasy, and the growing tension between Musk's ambitions and Tesla's realities.</p>
</details>

我们将探讨埃隆·马斯克庞大的商业帝国，现在还包括一家独立的**人工智能**（AI: Artificial Intelligence）公司，一些人指责这家公司从特斯拉抽走了人才和资源，这引发了严重的治理问题。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">We'll look at how Elon Musk's sprawling empire, now including a separate AI company that some are accusing of siphoning talent and resources away from Tesla, raises serious governance questions.</p>
</details>

我们还会问，特斯拉是否仍应被视为一家增长型公司，或者它只是一只产品线逐渐老化、首席执行官（CEO: Chief Executive Officer）总是在追逐下一个热门事物的“邪教股”。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">and will ask whether Tesla should still be considered a growth company or if it's just a cult stock with a fading product lineup and a CEO who's always on to the next hot thing.</p>
</details>

### 特斯拉全球销售数据分析

首先，我们应该看看数据。特斯拉的销量几乎在全球各地都在下降，而且下降幅度不小。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">First, we should look at the numbers. Tesla's sales are falling almost everywhere and not just slightly.</p>
</details>

在英国，7月份的注册量同比下降了60%。在德国，下降了55%。在瑞典，更是惊人的86%。在澳大利亚，特斯拉报告了该国所有主流品牌中最大的销量跌幅。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">In the UK, July registrations dropped 60% year-over-year. In Germany, they fell 55%. In Sweden, a staggering 86%. And in Australia, they've reported the biggest sales decline of any mainstream brand in the country.</p>
</details>

在欧洲各地，特斯拉已连续五个月出现下滑，2024年全年全球销量也呈下降趋势。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Across Europe, Tesla has posted five consecutive months of decline, and global sales for the full year of 2024 were also down.</p>
</details>

与此同时，比亚迪却在飙升。它现在是全球最大的电动汽车销售商，在销量和收入方面都已超越特斯拉。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">BYD, meanwhile, is surging. It's now the world's top EV seller, having overtaken Tesla in both unit sales and revenue.</p>
</details>

它的主导地位不再局限于中国或欧洲，而是全球性的。尽管美国市场对比亚迪实行了有效的禁令，特朗普和拜登政府征收的100%关税使得其销售具有竞争力的汽车几乎不可能，但比亚迪仍然取得了这些成就。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Its dominance is no longer confined to just China or Europe. It's global. And BYD has achieved this despite being effectively barred from the US market, where 100% tariffs imposed by both the Trump and Biden administrations make selling competitively priced cars nearly impossible.</p>
</details>

在中国，特斯拉7月份售出不到7万辆汽车，同比下降8.4%，延续了9个月的下降趋势。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">In China, Tesla sold just under 70,000 vehicles in July, down 8.4% year-over-year and continuing a 9-month downward trend.</p>
</details>

**Model Y**（特斯拉一款车型），曾是特斯拉的“皇冠上的明珠”，尽管他们为了税务目的令人困惑地声称它是一款**SUV**（Sport Utility Vehicle: 运动型多用途车），但它显然是一款紧凑型轿车，现在正逐渐被本地竞争对手超越。即使是改款版本也未能扭转下滑趋势。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The Model Y, once Tesla's crown jewel, which they confusingly claim is an SUV for tax purposes when it's clearly a compact car, is losing ground to local competitors. Even a refreshed version hasn't reversed the slide.</p>
</details>

在美国，特斯拉的销量可能比最初看起来更糟。虽然**Cox Automotive**（一家汽车市场研究公司）估计第一季度下降了8.6%，但**Electric**（一家电动汽车新闻网站）的弗雷德·兰帕德（Fred Lambert）进行的更准确的基于注册的分析表明，实际跌幅接近15%。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">In the United States, Tesla sales may be worse than they initially appear. While Cox Automotive estimated an 8.6% decline in the first quarter, more accurate registrationbased analysis done by Fred Lampard at Electric suggests that the real drop is closer to 15%.</p>
</details>

在加利福尼亚州，特斯拉美国销量的近40%来自该州，第二季度交付量同比下降21%，标志着连续第七个季度下滑。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">In California, which accounts for nearly 40% of Tesla's US sales, second quarter deliveries fell 21% year-over-year, marking the seventh straight quarter of decline.</p>
</details>

这意味着加州的销量早在埃隆·马斯克涉足政治之前就已经开始下降了。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">This means that sales in California were declining well before Elon Musk got involved in politics.</p>
</details>

更糟糕的是，特斯拉现在正面临加利福尼亚州机动车管理局（California Department of Motor Vehicles）的诉讼，指控其虚假宣传**Autopilot**（特斯拉的辅助驾驶系统）和**Full Self-Driving**（FSD: 全自动驾驶系统）功能，这可能威胁到其在该州销售汽车的能力。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">To top it off, Tesla is now facing a lawsuit from the California Department of Motor Vehicles over false advertising of its autopilot and full self-driving features, potentially threatening its ability to sell cars in the state.</p>
</details>

在全球范围内，特斯拉第一季度报告交付了近34万辆汽车，但库存水平正在上升，需求似乎正在减弱。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Globally, Tesla reported almost 340,000 deliveries in the first quarter, but inventory levels are rising and demand appears to be softening.</p>
</details>

尽管最近进行了改款，但特斯拉的新款Model Y没有积压订单，分析师们质疑特斯拉今年或明年是否还能实现销量增长。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Despite a recent faceelift, Tesla has no backlog for its new Model Y, and analysts are questioning whether Tesla can grow its sales at all this year or next.</p>
</details>

曾被吹捧为特斯拉最大创新的**Cybertruck**（特斯拉电动皮卡车型），很快就成了其最薄弱的环节。第二季度销量同比暴跌超过50%，这款卡车一直饱受召回、故障和嘲笑的困扰，甚至连忠实粉丝也对其可行性提出质疑。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The Cyber Truck, which was once hyped as Tesla's boldest innovation, has quickly become its weakest link. Sales plunged over 50% yearonear in the second quarter, and the truck has been plagued by recalls, breakdowns, and ridicule, with even loyal fans questioning its viability.</p>
</details>

### 马斯克天价薪酬与公司治理困境

本周早些时候，特斯拉董事会授予埃隆·马斯克9600万股股票，价值约290亿美元。公司将此方案描述为一项前瞻性激励，旨在留住和重新聚焦马斯克，因为他曾威胁称，除非获得更多控制权，否则将离开公司。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Earlier this week, Tesla's board awarded Elon Musk 96 million shares, which are worth around $29 billion. A package which the company framed as being a forward-looking incentive to retain and refocus Musk, who has threatened to leave the company unless he's given more control over it.</p>
</details>

这份方案使他成为历史上薪酬最高的首席执行官。为了对比他的薪酬，历史上第二高的首席执行官薪酬方案是授予黑石集团（Blackstone）联合创始人史蒂文·施瓦茨曼（Steven Schwarzman）的，他在2008年获得了总计13.9亿美元的薪酬。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">This package makes him the highest paid CEO in history. To put his pay in context, the second highest CEO pay package in history was awarded to Steven Schwarzman, the co-founder of Blackstone, who received $1.39 billion in total compensation in 2008.</p>
</details>

这笔支出主要归因于黑石集团当年的**首次公开募股**（IPO: Initial Public Offering）以及施瓦茨曼的股权。尽管当时市场动荡，但这笔支出发生在公司业绩强劲的一年。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">This payout was largely due to Blackstone's IPO that year and Schwarzman's equity stake. It happened during a year of strong performance despite the broader market turmoil.</p>
</details>

马斯克近300亿美元的薪酬方案不仅是施瓦茨曼的20多倍，而且是在特斯拉基本面正在走弱而非改善的时候出现的。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Musk's $30 billion pay package is not only more than 20 times larger than Schwarzman's, but it comes at a time when Tesla's fundamentals are weakening, not improving.</p>
</details>

**Quartz**（一家商业新闻网站）整理了一份幻灯片，列出了埃隆·马斯克之后历史上其他11位最高薪酬的首席执行官，其中只有两份薪酬方案超过了10亿美元。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Quartz put together a slideshow of the 11 other highest CEO payouts in history after Elon Musk's, and only two of these pay packages came to more than a billion dollars.</p>
</details>

有趣的是，历史上薪酬最高的首席执行官所在的一些公司要么不盈利，要么利润微薄。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Amusingly, a number of the companies with the highest paid CEOs in history were either unprofitable or barely profitable.</p>
</details>

马斯克的高薪与其他标志性创始人形成鲜明对比。比尔·盖茨（Bill Gates）、沃伦·巴菲特（Warren Buffett）、杰夫·贝佐斯（Jeff Bezos）和马克·扎克伯格（Mark Zuckerberg）等人物历史上都只领取适度的薪水，甚至不领薪水，而是依靠他们在所创立企业中的所有权股份来获得持续的激励。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Musk's high pay stands in stark contrast to other iconic founders. Figures like Bill Gates, Warren Buffett, Jeff Bezos, and Mark Zuckerberg have historically taken modest salaries, or none at all, and relied on their ownership stakes in the businesses that they founded for ongoing motivation.</p>
</details>

巴菲特以每年给自己支付10万美元而闻名，而扎克伯格自2013年以来每年只领取1美元的薪水。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Buffett famously paid himself $100,000 per year, while Zuckerberg has taken a $1 a year salary since 2013.</p>
</details>

这种薪酬模式反映了创始人之间更广泛的规范，他们通常由股权而非薪水驱动。风险投资家（Venture Capitalists）通常将适度的薪水视为创始人对其业务信心和对长期增长承诺的信号。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">This compensation model reflects a broader norm among founders who are typically motivated by equity rather than paychecks. Venture capitalists often view modest salaries as being a signal of a founders's confidence in their business and commitment to long-term growth.</p>
</details>

与此形成对比的是，埃隆·马斯克尽管是特斯拉最大的股东，却一再要求额外的**股权激励**（equity-based compensation），甚至威胁称，如果不能获得更多控制权，他将离开公司。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">In contrast to this, Elon Musk, despite being Tesla's largest shareholder, has repeatedly demanded additional equity-based compensation, even threatening to leave the company if not granted more control.</p>
</details>

他290亿美元的薪酬方案不仅因其规模而引人注目，也因其背离了创始人的常规做法。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">His $29 billion pay package stands out not just for its size, but for its departure from founder norms.</p>
</details>

特斯拉董事会将这份方案描述为“激励和聚焦马斯克的关键第一步”。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</p>
<p class="english-text">Tesla's board have described this package as being a critical first step to energize and focus Musk.</p>
</details>

我不确定接下来的步骤会是什么，但这份奖励将马斯克在特斯拉的持股比例从不到13%提高到约16%。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Now, I'm not sure what the following steps will be, uh, but the award increases Musk's stake in Tesla from under 13% to about 16% of the company.</p>
</details>

它对马斯克的唯一要求是，他必须在特斯拉担任高级领导职务两年。没有要求他投入更多时间在公司上，他只是不能辞职。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And the only requirement it puts on him is that he has to stay in a senior leadership role at Tesla for two more years. There's no requirement that he devote any more time to the company. He just can't quit.</p>
</details>

马斯克被推翻的2018年薪酬方案曾赋予他公司20%的股份。他曾表示，如果他要推进特斯拉的人工智能和机器人能力，他需要拥有至少25%的股份。而这些正是2025年最重要的流行词。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Musk's overturn 2018 compensation package had entitled him to 20% of the company. And he has said that he needs to own at least 25% if he's going to advance Tesla's artificial intelligence and robotics capabilities. And those are the most important buzzwords of 2025.</p>
</details>

他说25%的控制权足以阻止激进投资者将他赶下台，就像他几年前对推特（Twitter，现称**X Corp**）管理层所做的那样。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">He says that 25% would be enough control to prevent an activist investor from ousting him like he did to the management of Twitter a few years ago.</p>
</details>

尽管马斯克声称他对财富不感兴趣，只想要特斯拉的控制权，这将使他能够拯救人类，但近年来，他已出售了价值数十亿美元的特斯拉股票。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Despite his claim that he's not interested in wealth and only wants control of Tesla, which will allow him to save humanity, in recent years, Musk has sold billions of dollars worth of Tesla stock.</p>
</details>

他成功地在2022年末特斯拉股价接近历史高点时，出售了价值75亿美元的特斯拉股票，就在一份导致股价暴跌的销售报告发布之前。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">He managed to sell $7.5 billion dollars worth of Tesla shares near its all-time high in late 2022, right before a sales report that sent the price of the stock plunging.</p>
</details>

他的新薪酬方案被推销给特斯拉投资者，作为2018年560亿美元奖励的应急计划，该奖励因“过度且披露不充分”而被特拉华州法院否决。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
