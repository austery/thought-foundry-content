### 流行专辑发布与交通事故的关联

**Tracy Mumford**: 最后，**哈佛医学院**研究人员的一项研究发现，在大牌歌手发布专辑的日子（想想**泰勒·斯威夫特**或 **Bad Bunny**），交通事故死亡人数会上升。

他们查看了 2017 年至 2022 年的碰撞数据，并将这些数据与该时期内播放量最高的 10 张专辑的发布日期进行了对比，包括斯威夫特、**Drake**、**Kendrick Lamar**、**Harry Styles** 等。他们发现，在专辑发布的当天，交通事故死亡人数增加了近 15%。为了确保这不是偶然现象，他们将发布日期的数据与随机日期或往年同日的数据进行了对比。他们还排除了季节性或节假日旅行的因素。

一位研究人员打赌，发布日之所以危险，是因为听新专辑需要一系列**物理动作**：解锁手机、打开 App、滚动曲目列表、点击特定歌曲。这些都会分散司机的注意力。之前的研究也显示，在特定的日子（如**报税日**）交通死亡人数也会高于平时，可能是因为压力和分心。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Tracy Mumford**: And finally, a study from researchers at Harvard Medical School has found that on days with big album releases, think Taylor Swift or Bad Bunny, traffic fatalities go up. They looked at crash data from 2017 to 2022, the last year that had full stats when they started their analysis, and they overlaid that crash data with the release dates of the 10 most streamed albums in that period. Swift, Drake, Kendrick Lamar, Harry Styles, etc. They found that traffic fatalities increased nearly 15% on the days when the albums dropped. To try to ensure it wasn't an anomaly or related to something else. They did things like compare crash data on each release date to randomly selected dates or the same date, different year. They also ruled out seasonal or holiday travel as a contributing factor. One of the researchers said he would bet the danger on release days is because of the physical task of listening to a new album, unlocking the phone, opening an app, scrolling through a track list, tapping a specific song. Those are all things that could distract a driver. There has been previous research into things like this, whether certain days can correlate to more danger on the road.
