### 挪威模式：青少年体育与多元发展

**Nicola Tangan**: 有一种东西叫做**挪威模式**。那是什么？

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Nicola Tangan**: There is something called the Norwegian model. What is that?

</details>

**Anak Pensor**: 嗯，那实际上是我们对待青少年体育的方式。我们不允许在12岁之前公布成绩名单，或者只给一个人颁奖。所以如果我得到了奖品，你也会得到，即使我可能比你快。这也有助于我们思考，这些都是孩子。他们应该享受玩耍的乐趣，而不是在很小的时候就被挑出来作为天才。我认为这是为什么我们能够让很多青少年长期参与体育运动的一个关键因素。这样我们就有更多的人才可供选择。而且我们是一个小国，我们需要照顾每一个愿意参与的人。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Anak Pensor**: Well, that's actually the way we treat our youth sports. And that we're not allowed to have result lists or give only prices to one person up to the age of 12. So if I get the price, you get the price even though I might be faster than you. And that kind of also helps us think that these are children. They are supposed to have fun to play, not to be singled out as talent at an early age. And I think that's a key element in why we are able to have a lot of youth doing sports for a long time. And then we have more to pick from. And also, we are a tiny country. We need to take care of everyone who wants to.

</details>
