<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Lisa Freriedman**: Sure. It's called the endangerment finding and you can think of it like the spine of America's ability to regulate climate pollutants. </details>

</details>

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Lisa Freriedman**: and therefore should be regulated

</details>

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Michael Bubaro**: and therefore should be regulated.
