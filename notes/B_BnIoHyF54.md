### 宏观预警：劳动力市场加速走弱与降息预期升温

**劳动力市场**正在释放明显的放缓信号。私人咨询机构 Challenger 的最新报告显示，企业裁员意愿大幅上升，裁员规模可能达到多年来的高点。这一趋势得到了官方数据的印证：**职位空缺数**（Jolts）意外下降，每个求职者对应的职位空缺已跌破疫情前水平。此前，美联储理事 **Waller** 曾因担忧劳动力市场的下行风险而提议降息 25 个基点，当时的“少数派”意见如今正成为现实。

由于美国是典型的**服务业驱动型经济**，劳动力市场的疲软将直接导致薪资增长放缓，进而对**服务业通胀**产生下行压力。这种连锁反应意味着，通胀指数可能超预期回落，从而迫使美联储采取比目前市场定价（两次降息）更为激进的宽松政策。受政府停摆影响，原定于周五公布的**非农就业报告**（Non-farm Payrolls）将推迟至下周三。

Starting with the labor market. So last week we had Challenger report something that was pretty surprising. So Challenger is a private consulting company that goes and pulls companies about their intentions to hire or fire. Now according to them, businesses have significantly ramped up their intention to lay off workers. In fact, the total amount of announced layoffs could be as high as it's been in many years. Now that seems to be corroborated by official data that was announced a few days later. The Jolts report that shows that job openings surprisingly decreased. Now job openings per worker looking for a job has now declined to levels that are below pre-pandemic. So it's very clear that the labor market continues to soften. 

Now recall at the last Fed meeting, Governor Waller dissented and wanted a 25 basis point cut. The rationale he gave was that he was hearing from his contacts that everyone is thinking about laying off workers and so there was significant downside risk to the labor market. Direction seems very clear. The labor market is softening and if that's the case that also suggests downside risk to inflation. The US is largely a services-driven economy. If you have a weak labor market, then wage growth will decelerate and that suggests downward pressure on service inflation, which of course implies downward pressure on the broader inflation indexes. Together, weaker labor market, weaker inflation suggests that we'll probably get more Fed cuts than the two cuts priced into the market.

### AI 范式转移：从“基建狂热”到“SaaS 毁灭者”

科技股本周遭遇重创，市场对 **AI 投资**的逻辑发生了微妙而深刻的转变。过去，投资者倾向于奖励增加 AI 资本开支（CAPEX）的公司，认为这是通往未来的门票。然而，当 **Amazon** 宣布今年将投入 2000 亿美元用于 AI 基建时，股价却暴跌了 10%。这表明市场开始重新审视巨大的投入能否转化为利润，以及这是否会蚕食原本用于回购股份的现金流。

更具破坏性的信号来自 **Anthropic** 推出的新一代 AI 模型。该模型展示了强大的**直觉编程**（Vibe Coding）能力，允许非专业人员快速构建自定义软件。这直接威胁到了 **Salesforce** 和 **Adobe** 等 **软件即服务**（Software as a Service: 通过互联网提供软件订阅的商业模式）巨头的根基。如果企业能以极低成本定制化办公系统，那么昂贵的标准化 SaaS 服务将失去吸引力。这种恐慌甚至蔓延到了**私募信贷**（Private Credit）市场，因为如 **Ares Capital** 等大型机构约四分之一的资产流向了软件行业，AI 可能带来的行业蚕食正引发信贷市场的连锁去杠杆。

The second thing we have to talk about, of course, is the volatility and the equity markets. And this was largely centered in tech. Collectively the mag 7 companies are investing hundreds of billions in the AI buildup. In the past, the market rewarded seeing that the companies are investing in AI. Its new Claude version which apparently can do a lot of vibe coding really well that allows people who are not trained programmers to be able to vibe code things really easily. Basically customized software that could potentially replace software that their companies currently use, let's say things like document review, back office stuff, payroll accounting. So companies that currently specialize in things like that like Salesforce or Adobe, they saw their stock price decline pretty notably. The thinking is that if a company can use an AI bot to create customized software to suit their needs at a very low price, why would they be using the services of these SaaS companies. This also bled into the credit markets. Ares Capital Corporation has almost a quarter of their assets in loans to software companies. The market is worried that AI could cannibalize other software companies and that could present downside risk to the loans.

### 加密货币：消失的“抗通胀”属性与信心危机

**比特币**（Bitcoin）本周表现极度波动，一度跌破 6 万美元大关，回吐了自特朗普（Donald Trump）胜选以来的所有涨幅。尽管特朗普政府履行了多项亲加密货币的承诺——包括通过《天才法案》（Genius Act）建立**稳定币**监管框架，甚至提议建立战略性比特币储备，但市场情绪依然低迷。

目前加密货币正面临一场**信心危机**。长期以来，支持者认为比特币是**法币贬值交易**（Debasement Trade: 通过购买实物资产来规避货币超发导致的购买力下降风险）的避险工具。然而，在黄金、白银甚至铜铝等传统抗通胀资产大幅飙升、美元走软的背景下，比特币却逆势下跌，未能展现出其宣称的对冲属性。此外，由于比特币持有者通常也是科技股的投资者，科技行业的动荡引发了跨资产的**去杠杆**（Deleveraging）需求，进一步压制了币价。

Last thing that I want to talk about of course is crypto. This last week we saw tremendous volatility in Bitcoin.
