### 涡轮发动机的工作原理

这是一个喷气发动机，具体来说是一个**涡轮发动机**（Turboan Engine: 一种利用燃气涡轮驱动风扇和压缩机，并通过喷射燃气产生推力的喷气发动机）。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

<p class="english-text">At the front is this giant fan.</p>

### 效率与设计权衡

一些战斗机确实是这样做的，这使得它们的发动机非常强大，但效率也极低。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Well, some fighter jets do exactly this, and it makes for very powerful engines, but they're also horribly inefficient.</p>
</details>

要了解原因，请记住推动飞机前进的**冲量**（Impulse: 力在时间上的积累效应，等于动量的变化量）等于空气向后动量的变化。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">To see why, remember that the impulse pushing the plane forward is equal to the change in the momentum of the air backwards.</p>
</details>

所以你有选择。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">So, you've got options.</p>
</details>

例如，你可以以一半的速度推动两倍的空气向后，或者以两倍的速度推动一半的空气向后。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">For example, you could push twice as much air back half as fast, or you could push half as much air back twice as fast.</p>
</details>

两者都会产生完全相同的冲量，但空气的**动能**（Kinetic Energy: 物体因运动而拥有的能量）与速度的平方成正比。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Both will generate the exact same impulse, but the kinetic energy of the air is proportional to V squared.</p>
</details>

因此，在第二种情况下加速空气需要四倍的能量。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">So it takes four times as much energy to speed up the air in the second case.</p>
</details>

而其中很多能量都浪费在排气中。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And a lot of that energy is just wasted in the exhaust.</p>
</details>

所以理想情况下，你希望以尽可能小的速度变化推动尽可能多的空气向后。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">So ideally, you want to push as much air backwards as possible with only a small change in velocity.</p>
</details>

这就是为什么多年来喷气机变得越来越大。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">That's why jets have gotten bigger and bigger over the years.</p>
</details>

增加的**旁通比**（Bypass Air: 喷气发动机中不经过燃烧室，直接由风扇推动的空气）还有一个额外的好处，就是它包围着热排气，从而降低了喷气机发出的噪音。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">and the increasing fraction of bypass air has the added benefit that it surrounds the hot exhaust gases and that reduces noise coming away from the jet.</p>
</details>

但在发动机效率方面，还有一个主要因素，那就是温度。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">But there is another major factor when it comes to engine efficiency and that is temperature.</p>
</details>

在35,000英尺左右的巡航高度，外部空气温度约为零下55摄氏度，而发动机内部则超过1500摄氏度。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">At cruising altitudes around 35,000 ft the outside air is around55° C while inside the engine it's over 1,500°.</p>
</details>

发动机内部的高温高压气体想要膨胀到外部更冷、更低压的空气中。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The hot high-pressure gas inside the engine wants to expand into the much colder, lower pressure air outside.</p>
</details>

正是这种差异让发动机将热能转化为有用的功。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">It's that difference that lets the engine turn heat into useful work.</p>
</details>

但任何热机能从中获得的功都有一个基本限制，这被称为**卡诺效率**（Carno Efficiency: 理想热机在给定高温和低温热源之间所能达到的最大热效率）。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">But there's a fundamental limit to how much work any heat engine can get from that. It's called the carno efficiency.</p>
</details>

它等于1减去外部冷空气的温度除以燃烧室内部热气体的温度。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">It's equal to 1 minus the temperature of the cold outside air divided by the temperature of the hot gas inside the combustion chamber.</p>
</details>

因此，从这一点来看，你可以通过两种方式提高发动机的效率：要么在空气更冷的地方飞行，要么提高燃烧室的温度。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</p>
<p class="english-text">So looking at this, you can improve the efficiency of the engine in two ways. Either fly where the air is colder or raise the temperature in the combustion chamber.</p>
</details>

然而，这样做的一个问题是，它将喷气发动机内部变成了我们建造过的最恶劣的环境之一，机械必须在这种环境中生存。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">One problem with that though is that it turned the inside of a jet engine into one of the harshest environments we have ever built in which machinery has to survive.</p>
</details>

让涡轮叶片在发动机内部保持完整且不受影响，就像把冰块放进烤箱，调到最大，然后去上班，八小时后回来，发现它仍然完全冻在烤箱里一样。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">To keep a turbine blade whole and unaffected within an engine is like putting an ice cube inside your oven, turning up to max, leaving for work, coming back after an eight hour shift, and finding it still completely frozen in the oven.</p>
</details>

这就是我们必须在那个发动机内部尝试做到的。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">That's what we've got to try and do within that engine.</p>
</details>

>> 这听起来很荒谬。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">>> It sounds absurd.</p>
</details>

涡轮叶片不仅置身于超过1500摄氏度的气流中，它们还以每分钟12,500转的速度旋转，每个叶片的尖端以近1900公里/小时的速度划破空气。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Not only do the turbine blades sit in a stream of gas that's over,500° C, they're also spinning at 12,500 RPM with the tip of each blade slicing through the air at nearly 1,900 kmh.</p>
</details>

现在，每个叶片都想直线飞行，但它被迫旋转成一个圆圈，这意味着必须有某种力量不断地将其向内拉。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Now, every blade wants to fly straight, but it's forced to spin in a circle, which means something has to be constantly pulling it inwards.</p>
</details>

这就是向心力。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">That's the centrial force.</p>
</details>

如果你取一个代表性的300克高压涡轮叶片，并以那个速度和半径运行，它必须以相当于20公吨重量的力向内拉。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">If you take a representative 300 g high-pressure turbine blade and run it at that speed and radius, it has to be pulled inwards with a force equal to the weight of 20 metric tons.</p>
</details>

这大约相当于两辆伦敦双层巴士的重量，在每个叶片旋转时拉扯着它，而它们都在炽热地发光。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">That's roughly the weight of two London double-decker buses tugging on each blade as it spins, all while they're glowing hot.</p>
</details>

更糟糕的是，在这些温度下，氧气会与叶片本身的金属发生反应。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">To make matters worse, at these temperatures, oxygen wants to react with the metal of the blades itself.</p>
</details>

最重要的是，冲过发动机的空气通常携带灰尘、沙子和污染物，这些都会损坏和侵蚀内部表面。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And on top of all that, the air rushing through the engine often carries dust, sand, and pollutants that can damage and erode the surfaces inside.</p>
</details>

然而，这些叶片必须在这种惩罚下生存数万飞行小时，而不会变形、开裂或失效。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And somehow these blades have to survive this punishment for tens of thousands of flight hours without deforming, cracking, or failing.</p>
</details>

它们真正决定了发动机的效率能达到多高，因为你不能让发动机热到叶片无法承受的程度。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">They really determine how efficient you can make the engine because you can't make the engine so hot that the blades can't withstand that temperature.</p>
</details>

所以它们决定了燃烧室的最高温度，从而决定了喷气发动机所能实现的最高效率。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">So they determine the maximum temperature of the combustion chamber and therefore the maximum efficiency you can realize with a jet engine.</p>
</details>

那么，什么样的金属才能在这种条件下生存呢？
