### 供应链本地化与跨代创新

由于可以从本地采购，产品开发的速度大大加快。国外的产品很多是标准化的现货方案，而**本地合作伙伴**更愿意与你合作，开发更符合我们需求的**定制化方案**。这不仅让速度更快，也让我们有能力提供更契合智能手机需求的定制化体验，从而更好地满足消费者的愿望。

### 智能家居与 IoT 的互联体验

**Nicolai Tangen**: **物联网（IoT）** 以及与家电的互联对你们有多重要？

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Nicolai Tangen**: How important has the internet of things or your connectivity with home appliances been?

</details>

**林世伟**: 非常重要。现代用户喜欢智能化的解决方案。像空调、洗衣机、冰箱这些技术已经存在了三五十年，很久没有改变了。我们提供的是**智能方案**，你可以像手机一样对这些设备进行 **OTA（越空）软件升级**，而不需要购买新机器。

此外，通过将一切连接在一起，整个生活方式会变得更聪明。智能设备不仅仅是能用手机远程控制，而是能理解用户、理解用户的行为，并每天提供**定制化的智能方案**。

例如，**智能门锁**。它不仅能让你看到门外的情况，提供专门的密码以保护隐私，如果你将它与其他设备互联，当你打开门锁时，空调会自动开启。这就是我们认为用户需要的智能解决方案。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Alain Lam**: It is important because I think in this world a lot of our users like intelligent solutions right. Think about air conditioners, washing machines, refrigerators. The technology have existed for 30-50 years but that hasn't changed for a long time. What we offer to our users is a smart solutions. Now you can upgrade your software over the air in your devices as opposed to buying a new machines. Plus the fact is can you make your whole lifestyle, whole ecosystem much smarter by connecting everything together so that they can do things for you. Not just using your smartphone as a remote control but smart intelligence solutions that they understand who you are they understand your behavior and they can try to offer customized solutions for you every day. For example, smart lock. Not just you can look at who's outside of your door, you can offer specialized password for people to enter your apartment so that you don't leak your secrecy. But also, if you interconnect it with other devices at home, for example, when you open your smart lock, your AC will automatically be switched on, right? And those are the smart solutions that we like to think that our users need.

</details>

### 小米汽车：三年造车与“中国速度”

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Alain Lam**: Correct. We decided to get into the car industry in 2021 and then we launched our first car in 2024.

</details>

**Nicolai Tangen**: 这怎么可能？

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Nicolai Tangen**: How is that possible?

小米在进入造车领域之前，已经投资了一些中国的电动汽车厂商，有观察和帮助他们发展的经验。当我们决定自己下场时，我们得出的结论是：如果你相信**电动汽车（EV）**将成为另一件**消费电子产品**，那么我们在软硬件集成、供应链管理方面的丰富经验就派上用场了。虽然汽车在安全性等方面有所不同，但中国的电动汽车供应链已经非常成熟。

我们的重要理念是：**投入 10 倍的精力来打造第一款车**。我们没有盲目地同时开发多款车型，而是倾尽全力打造这一款单品。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Alain Lam**: So a little bit over less than three years we launched our first car, we designed it and then we built a factory as well. So China speed. At the beginning the car industry to us is not that foreign. Xiaomi as a company has already invested in some of the EV manufacturers in China before. So we have an experience of seeing them develop. But then we decided to do it ourselves. The ultimate conclusion is that if you believe that a car, a EV is going to be another piece of consumer electronics and that's something that we have a lot of experience in whether it is managing the software hardware integration or managing the supply chain. Obviously car is a little bit of a different animal because you need to consider about safety but again the supply chain in China has been relatively well-developed on the EV industry. What we said was, can we spend 10 times more investments to build the first car. Instead of making multiple cars we decided to spend all of our efforts on one single car.

The rest I think we can rely on other people. We have around 3,000 people to develop the first car which is probably 10 times more than other peers because our chairman has the philosophy that we want to spend a lot of effort initially to catch up with our peers.

### 手机与汽车制造的异同

**Nicolai Tangen**: 造手机和造车有哪些相似之处？

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Nicolai Tangen**: What are the similarities between making a phone and a car?

</details>

**林世伟**: 有很多不同。在 2024 年之前，我们没有自己的手机工厂，而是外包给**富士康**或 **BYD**。我们负责设计、核心技术和营销。但对于汽车业务，我们在北京建设了一座全新的**大规模现代化工厂**，这是我们首次亲身管理大规模制造。

相似之处在于，**软件定义硬件**。在电动汽车中，软件的重要性日益增加，就像手机一样。在手机里，我们将大量芯片堆叠在极小的空间；在车里，我们也需要管理大量组件，尤其是管理电池效能和驾驶软件，这与手机业务非常相似。此外，两者的供应链管理也有很多重叠，许多供应商是共通的。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Alain Lam**: Some similarities, a lot of differences obviously. In the phone industry before 2024 we did not have a smartphone factory. We were using outsourcing it to the Foxconn of the world, the BYD of the world. For the car business we undertook the task of building an entire new factory in Beijing. That's our first real large scale factory. But there's a lot of similarities because increasingly the software drive the hardware in the EV just like they're in the smartphone. In smartphone we stack up a lot of chipset into a very small form factor, in the car is similar we have to stack up a lot of components. Especially in the EV world the software becomes very important. How do you manage battery power? How do you manage the software to drive the car in a safe manner? Managing the supply chain is also very important. We have a lot of suppliers that are the same between the smartphone business as well as the EV business.

</details>

How do I link it with all of our ecosystem products? I can create not just a EV but also a smart life ecosystem beyond the EV.
