### 自主化技术对造船业的颠覆

**主持人**: 你是如何将 **Seronics** 的自主化技术与海军“不抛弃任何一个伙伴”的信念转化为合同的？自主化技术的突破又是如何让你为海军生产出更多、更快的产品的？

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Host**: How have you translated this alignment between Seronics's autonomy and the Navy's no man left behind ethos into contracts? and and how does how do autonomy breakthroughs actually allow you to build more faster products for the Navy?

</details>

**Dino**: 这个问题我会再回过头来详细说。我坚信，自主化开启的是以前从未存在过的能力。当然，战争在某些环节仍然需要人类的参与，但我亲眼见过，如果你有能力派机器人去，你就永远不应该派人去。你就是不应该这么做。所以，让我们利用**机器人技术**，在可以的地方保护人类生命。

自主化真正的开启钥匙是**速度和规模**。如果我坐在这里告诉部长，我们将建造过去 50 年一直在建的那种船，或者建造和中国一样的船，只是做得更好、更快、更便宜，那他应该礼貌地请我离开。你必须弯曲经济成本曲线。你必须造船更便宜，造船更快速。

你唯一能拨动的两个杠杆是材料成本和劳动力成本。我们买钢材的价格不可能比中国便宜，所以你必须对船只进行**首要原则 (First Principles)** 的重新设计，从根本上减少钢材的使用。你怎么做到的？你通过**软件自主化**和**数字化**来进行建造。

劳动力工时也是一样。我们获得的每小时人工费率不可能比中国低。所以，让我们把造船速度提得更快。例如，我们的第一艘 **Marauder** 船大约消耗了 5 万个工时。为了让大家对数量级有个概念，我先声明这有点像苹果和橘子的类比，但一艘驱逐舰需要 700 万到 900 万个工时。

这就是自主化所开启的规模和速度。因为你可以重新设计整个平台，使其建造起来简单得多。然后你就拥有了所需的规模化能力，从而让尽可能多的人远离战斗场景。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Dino**: So, that's one I'll come back to. What autonomy enables is, and I'm a I'm a huge believer in this, we're unlocking capability that didn't exist before. Like, warfare will require humans involvement at some point, but I've seen it. And you should never send a human if you have the ability to send a robot. you just shouldn't do it. So, let's leverage robotics and protect human life where we can. The real unlock to autonomy is speed and scale. Right? If I was sitting up here telling the secretary how we're going to build the same ships that we've been building for the last 50 years or the same ships that they're building in China, we're just going to do it better, faster, and cheaper. I mean, he should just politely ask me to leave. You have to bend the economic cost curve. You have to build ships cheaper. You have to build ships faster. The only two levers you have to pull are material costs and labor costs. We're not going to buy steel cheaper than they're buying it in China. So, you have to take a first principles design to the ship and just fundamentally use less steel. How do you do that? You build for software autonomy and digitization. Same thing on labor hours. We're not going to acquire a per hour labor rate cheaper than they're paying in China. So, let's build ships much faster. For example, Marauders using is about a 50,000 labor hour on our first ship. Just for order of magnitude, put it in frame of reference. Apples and oranges comparison here. I'm going to acknowledge that up front, but a destroyer is 7 to 9 million labor hours. So that's the scale and speed that autonomy unlocks because you can redesign the whole platform and make it much simpler to build and then you build the then you have the capability at the scale that you need to keep as many people as possible out of combat scenarios.
