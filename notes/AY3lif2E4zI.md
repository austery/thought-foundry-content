### Claude 加速私募股权交易流程

让我们来看看**Claude**（Anthropic公司开发的人工智能助手）如何将**私募股权**（Private Equity: 对非上市公司股权进行投资，旨在通过改善经营或市场运作实现高额回报）的**交易流程**（Deal Flow: 指投资机构在特定时期内评估和执行潜在投资项目的数量）从数日缩短至数小时。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Let's look at how Claude accelerates private equity deal flow from days to hours.</p>
</details>

我们将以**Riverside Partners**（一家私募股权公司）的合伙人Sarah，以及**Walt Capital**（一家私募股权公司）的副总裁Jen为例。他们正在处理**Horizon Health Group**（一家价值8500万美元的医疗服务公司）的交易。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">We'll follow Sarah, an associate at Riverside Partners on the Southside, and Jen, a VP at Walt Capital on the Bi side. They're working on Horizon Health Group, an $85 million healthcare services business.</p>
</details>

### 交易撮合简报的快速生成

Sarah需要一份**交易撮合简报**（DO Teaser: 一种简短的、非保密的介绍性文件，用于吸引潜在买家对某项交易的兴趣），并请求Claude利用其“交易撮合简报技能”来创建一份客户就绪的演示文稿。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Sarah needs a DO teaser and asks Claude to create a client-ready presentation using the DO teaser skill.</p>
</details>

Claude随即在**Ignite**（一个数据平台）中搜索，找到了财务数据、客户分析和运营数据，并从中提取了收入、利润率、客户留存指标和设施详情。不到两分钟，Sarah就得到了一份专业的交易撮合简报，可以立即发送。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Claude searches Ignite, finding financials, customer analysis, and operations data, and extracting revenue, margins, retention metrics, and facility details. In under two minutes, Sarah's got a professional teaser ready to send.</p>
</details>

### 初步筛选与深度尽职调查

这份简报送达了Jen的收件箱。她请求Claude筛选这份简报，并利用其**杠杆收购模型**（LBO Skill: Leveraged Buyout Skill，指利用高比例债务融资收购公司的专业技能）来构建一个杠杆收购模型。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The teaser lands in Jenzenbox. She asks Claude to screen it and build a leveraged bio model using the LPO skill.</p>
</details>

Claude从**SharePoint**（微软的协作平台）中提取投资标准，并从简报中抽取数据。几分钟内，Jen就收到了一份全面的筛选备忘录，发现Horizon Health似乎符合所有条件。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Claude pulls from SharePoint for investment criteria and extracts data from the teaser. Within minutes, Jen gets a comprehensive screening memo and sees that Horizon Health seems to check all the boxes.</p>
</details>

但Claude进行了更深入的尽职调查。它搜索了Ignite的数据室，发现**Blue Cross Regional**（一家医疗保险公司，在此案例中是Horizon Health Group的重要客户）贡献了18%的收入，而其合同将于2025年12月到期。此外，还有28%的收入合同包含短期终止条款。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">But Claude has deeper diligence. It searches the Ignite datarum to find that Blue Cross Regional is 18% of revenue with a contract that expires December 2025. 28% of revenue has short-term termination clauses.</p>
</details>

这是在初步审查中可能不会被发现的风险。结论是：有条件通过，但Blue Cross的续约需要尽快敲定。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">This is risk that wouldn't have been picked up at a claim. The verdict conditional pass that BlueCrust renewal needs to be locked down.</p>
</details>

### 财务建模与压力测试

随后，Jen开始审视这些数字。Claude在几分钟内就建立了一个完整的模型，而这通常需要分析师花费数小时才能完成。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Nogen looks at the numbers. Claude built a complete model in minutes, which would have taken an analyst hours.</p>
</details>

模型显示，收购价格为**EBITDA**（Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, and Amortization: 息税折旧摊销前利润，衡量公司盈利能力的指标）的11倍，五倍杠杆，包含五年现金流预测和债务摊销计划。其中包括乐观情景和基准情景。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Purchase price at 11 times EBITDA, five times leverage with five year cashflow projections and a debt amortization. An upside and base case.</p>
</details>

基准情景显示，**内部收益率**（IRR: Internal Rate of Return，衡量投资项目盈利能力的指标）为26%，**资金倍数**（Money Multiple: 投资收回的倍数）为3倍，远高于20%的**门槛收益率**（Hurdle Rate: 投资项目必须达到的最低回报率）。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">This case shows 26% IR and a 3x money multiple, well above the 20% hurdle.</p>
</details>

Jen与Claude进行压力测试，要求在**Excel**（微软的电子表格软件）中添加一个悲观情景。Claude立即构建了一个新的情景，其中包含较低的增长和利润率假设。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Jen stress tests with Claude, asking to add a downside case in Excel. Claude immediately builds a new scenario with lower growth and margin assumptions.</p>
</details>

Jen实时查看Claude的工作，并验证了其假设。悲观情景下的内部收益率为19%，略低于门槛。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Jen sees Claude's work in real time and validates its assumptions. Downside yields 19% IR right after threshold.</p>
</details>

随后，她要求进行敏感性分析。Claude构建了一个矩阵，模拟8倍至12倍**退出倍数**（Exit Multiple: 投资退出时，公司估值相对于其EBITDA的倍数）。在10倍退出倍数下，各项数据与模型完全吻合。即使在8倍退出倍数下，回报也依然可接受。整体结果范围看起来不错。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">she asks for a sensitivity analysis. Claude builds a matrix, 8x to 12x exits. At 10x, the numbers tie exactly to the model. Even at 8x, the returns are acceptable. The fan of outcomes look good.</p>
</details>

### 投资建议与最终决策

此时是周一早上，Jen将在几小时后参加投资委员会会议。她请求Claude综合所有信息，并提出一份**意向书**（Letter of Intent, LOI: 投资方在正式交易协议前向目标公司发出的非约束性文件，表达收购意向）建议。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">It's Monday morning, and Jen has an investment committee meeting in a few hours. She asked Claude to synthesize everything with a letter of intent recommendation.</p>
</details>

几分钟内，Jen就收到了一份投资委员会演示文稿，建议以EBITDA的10至11倍价格推进意向书。Claude将合同风险标记为意向书签署前的先决条件。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">In minutes, Jen receives an investment committee deck with a recommendation to proceed with a letter of intent at 10 to 11 times EBITDA. Claude flags contract risks as pre-LOI conditions.</p>
</details>

基准情景产生26%的内部收益率，悲观情景为19%。意向书建议：如果Blue Cross的合同能够锁定，则以11倍EBITDA进行；否则回报将不确定。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Base case generates 26% IOR. Downside 19%. LOI recommendation, 11 times if Blue Cross is locked down. Takeoff return if not.</p>
</details>

Sarah在两分钟内完成了从数据室到交易撮合简报的制作。Jen则在48小时内从收到简报到形成投资建议。Claude通过阅读数据室文件，发现了重大的合同风险，并量化分析表明交易仍然可行。这意味着更快、更优质的尽职调查。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Sarah went from data room to teaser in two minutes. Jen took a teaser to an investment recommendation in 48 hours. Claude caught a material contract risk by reading the data room files, then quantified the deal still works. This is better diligence, faster.</p>
</details>
