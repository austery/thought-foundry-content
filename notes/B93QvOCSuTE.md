### 优先提升主动收入，而非被动收入

在你拥有百万美元之前，甚至不要考虑**被动收入**（Passive Income: 无需投入大量时间和精力即可持续获得的收入）。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Before you have a million dollars, don't even think about passive income.</p>
</details>

你根本没有资产可以用来赚取被动收入。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">You have no asset to actually earn passive income off of.</p>
</details>

你的时间最好花在学习如何赚取你的第一个百万美元上，而不是思考如何通过被动收入在你投资的本金上赚取百分比收益。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Your time is much better spent learning how do you get your first million dollars than it is through passive income and thinking how you're going to earn a percentage on the principal that you've invested.</p>
</details>

如果你每年赚取6万美元，在此基础上获得8%的收益并不能让你实现**财务自由**（Financial Freedom: 个人拥有足够的被动收入来支付生活开销，无需为金钱工作）。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">If you make $60,000 a year, 8% on that isn't gonna get you to financial freedom.</p>
</details>

不要去琢磨如何从8%的收益率提高到15%，因为你的本金仍然太少。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Don't figure out how to crack 8% to maybe I'm gonna get 15% returns because the principle is still so small.</p>
</details>

6万美元的本金实在太少了。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The 60,000 is so small.</p>
</details>

实际上，你应该学习如何将你的收入提高到16万美元。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Actually learn how do I make my income 160,000.</p>
</details>

但令人惊讶的是，一旦人们学会了这一点，当你达到这个目标时，你会想：“天哪，原来就这么简单！”那些我曾以为很特别的人，不过是多赚了10万美元而已。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">But what's wild once people learn how to do this, as soon as you get there, you're like, "Oh my gosh, if that's all it took." for these people that I used to think were special to earn an additional 100,000.</p>
</details>

现在我所要做的就是再次重复同样的过程，从我目前的水平达到赚取160万美元，实现10倍增长，这太棒了！

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And now all I have to do is do that same process again to go from where I'm at to be able to earn 1.6 million, do a 10X, sign me up.</p>
</details>

因此，被动收入在人生的某个阶段确实很棒，但你99%的时间应该专注于如何让你的第一个计划（即主动收入计划）顺利进行，并尽一切可能掌控好你的A计划。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And so passive income is great at a certain stage of life, but 99% of your time should be focused on how do I make the first plan go fricking right and do everything I can today to control plan A.</p>
</details>
