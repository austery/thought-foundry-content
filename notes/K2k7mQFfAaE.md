### 与亲近之人共事的价值

我认为与你亲近的人一起工作是被低估的。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">I think working with people you're close to is underrated.</p>
</details>

我觉得在我生命中所有重要的事业中，它们不仅是与他人一起完成的，更是与我非常亲近的人一起完成的，而且我曾拥有并希望拥有超越这些事业的持久关系。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">I feel like for all the ventures of any significance in my life, they've not only been with others, but been with other people that I'm very close to and where I had and would like to have an enduring relationship that outlives them.</p>
</details>

有时，人们会给出这样的建议：你不应该与朋友共事，或者不应该与你的伴侣一起工作等等。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And sometimes when here's the advice that you shouldn't work with friends or you shouldn't work with your partner or something like that.</p>
</details>

尽管所有这些事情都带有个人特质，并且每种可能的组合都有其独特的案例，但对我而言，这确实是一次非常有益的经历。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And all these things are idiosyncratic and there are instances of every possible permutation. but... but for me it's been a really rewarding experience.</p>
</details>
