### Michael Burry的AI做空逻辑：折旧与资本开支

最后，我们来聊一下AI，或者说“世纪大空头”是如何做空AI的。他背后的一个核心概念是会计准则中的“**折旧**”（Depreciation: 资产价值随时间推移而减少的会计处理）。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Finally, let's talk about AI, or how the "Big Short" is shorting AI. One of the core concepts behind his view is "depreciation" in accounting principles.</p>
</details>

通常来说，我们在做资本性开支（**Capex**：Capital Expenditure，资本支出，用于购买或升级固定资产）和运营开支（**Opex**：Operating Expenditure，运营支出，用于日常经营）时，其背后的概念是一样的，都是公司花钱。运营开支是你每年花多少就是多少，而资本性开支背后有一个比较“棘手”的地方。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Generally speaking, when we talk about capital expenditure (Capex) and operating expenditure (Opex), the underlying concept is the same: a company spends money. Operating expenditure is simply how much you spend each year, but capital expenditure has a trickier aspect.</p>
</details>

这意味着你现在花100块钱，这100块钱不会马上计入你今年的开支，因为它有可能分成5年、两年或15年去进行折旧。那么，到底是两年、五年还是15年呢？这背后“公说公有理，婆说婆有理”，于是就出现了观点上的不一致。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">This means that if you spend 100 yuan now, this 100 yuan will not immediately enter your expenses for this year, because it may be depreciated over 5 years, 2 years, or 15 years. So, should it be 2 years, 5 years, or 15 years? There are valid arguments from different perspectives, leading to disagreements in viewpoints.</p>
</details>

观点上的不一致会导致大家对这个假设的不一致，从而使得有些人认为整个市场中的这些假设过于乐观，有些人可能认为是过于悲观，或者合理。没有人会认为过于悲观。如果认为是过于乐观的话，就像Michael Burry一样，他认为过于乐观，那么整个市场里边就存在泡沫。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Disagreements in viewpoints lead to disagreements in assumptions, causing some to believe these assumptions in the market are overly optimistic, while others might consider them overly pessimistic, or reasonable. No one would consider them overly pessimistic. If they are considered overly optimistic, like Michael Burry, he believes they are overly optimistic, then there is a bubble in the entire market.</p>
</details>

举个例子，资本性开支。我现在花100块钱，如果我现在买入的**GPU**（Graphics Processing Unit: 图形处理器）在5年之后不值任何钱，那么每年的花费就是20块钱，对吧？每年你应该计入的折旧就是20块钱。如果它在4年之后就没有任何价值，那么你的折旧就应该是25块钱。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">For example, capital expenditure. If I spend 100 yuan now, and the GPU I buy now is worth nothing after 5 years, then the annual expense is 20 yuan, right?
