### 投资、交易与博弈的区别

**Tracy Alloway**: 你如何区分投资、交易和博弈（赌博）这三种行为？因为代币化股票等产品总是会引发这种紧张感。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Tracy Alloway**: I have one conceptual question, which is when you think about the difference between, I guess, investor trading and gambling, how would you differentiate those three activities?

</details>

**Vlad Tenev**: 我认为投资与交易的区别主要是**速度**。**投资**的心理模型是买入并长期持有，从不打算卖出，只想看着资产增值。**交易**则是看到一个有时效性的机会，买入并在机会消失前卖出。而**博弈**大多是由情感驱动的，比如因为我喜欢某个本地球队而下注，纯粹是为了娱乐。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Vlad Tenev**: I think that the difference between investing and trading is really one of velocity. Investing to me is the mental model is buying something and I never intend to sell it. Longer term. So, so it's sort of like accumulating assets and you intend to, to have them only grow. Trading is I'm going to move in and out because I see an opportunity. And that opportunity might not exist in one day or three months. But there's like a very particular thesis that I have that's time bound and systematic and, I think gambling is, I think gambling is like, mostly emotional, driven.

</details>

### 利益冲突与逆向选择

**Joe Weisenthal**: 传统的风险投资目标是赚钱，而 RVI 里的公司名字都很“性感”。我为什么不该认为这个组合是为了吸引散户关注而优化的，而不是为了最优回报？

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Joe Weisenthal**: It seems to me like, okay, the traditional VC fund, like you're going to optimize for making the most money, the best returns, including names that no one has ever heard of. Why should I not think that Robin Hood's portfolio has been optimized for retail awareness?

**Vlad Tenev**: 首先，我们的业绩是完全透明的，我们要履行**信托义务 (Fiduciary Obligation)**。你担心的其实是**逆向选择 (Adverse Selection)**：是不是只有那些急需钱的公司才会分给散户额度？事实恰恰相反，我们进入的都是竞争极其激烈的项目，我们必须努力争取配额。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Vlad Tenev**: Well, I mean, first of all, we are incented to make this firm, perform well, right? The returns will just be out there. And, you know, we have a fiduciary obligation. I think the biggest worry that you have with things like this is actually adverse selection, right? Is are you just going to get into the deals of the companies that, need the money and are desperate for.

</details>
