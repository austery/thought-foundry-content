### 赋能与成功的共享

有时候，我们倾向于只把人看作问题的一部分，却不把他们看作成功的一部分。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Sometimes we tend to make people part of the problem only, but we don't make people part of the success.</p>
</details>

我们很快会说：“这些是我的挑战”，但却不那么快地说：“是这个人移除了这个障碍，那个人移除了那个障碍。”

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">We're quick to say, "These were my challenges," but we're not quick to say, "But this person removed this barrier and this person removed this one."</p>
</details>

我们需要开始讲述这些故事。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">We need to start telling those stories.</p>
</details>

### Girls Fly Africa：五大赋能支柱

Girls Fly Africa 激励年轻人，特别是年轻女性，学习飞行。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Girls Fly Africa inspires young people, especially young women, to learn how to fly.</p>
</details>

我们的大多数学生来自从未接触过航空业的农村地区，我们的项目设置旨在考虑他们可能遇到的挑战。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Most of our students, they come from rural areas that have never been exposed to the industry, and our program is set up in a way that it kind of looks at what are some of the challenges they might encounter.</p>
</details>

我们确定了五个方面。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">We identify five areas.</p>
