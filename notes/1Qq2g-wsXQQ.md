### 中国对伊朗的支持与稀土依赖

**特雷西·芒福德**: **伊朗**是中国在中东最亲密的伙伴，购买了伊朗约80%的石油出口。自战争爆发以来，中国向伊朗提供了情报，并让其使用了一颗跟踪该地区美军位置的间谍卫星。就在特朗普飞往中国时，《纽约时报》从美国官员处获悉，中国公司一直在讨论向伊朗出售武器。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Tracy Mumford**: Iran is China's closest partner in the Middle East and the country buys about 80% of Iran's oil exports. Since the beginning of the war, China has given Iran intelligence, plus access to a spy satellite that has tracked the positions of US forces in the region. And just as Trump was flying to China, the Times learned from US officials that Chinese companies have been discussing selling weapons to Iran.

</details>

**特雷西·芒福德**: 值得注意的是，伊朗战争实际上使美国与中国的联系更加紧密。这是因为中国主导了**稀土矿物**的全球生产，这是美国从巡航导弹到战斗机等武器的关键组成部分。一位前美国贸易官员告诉《时报》：“每一枚射向伊朗的导弹，都让我们在短期内更加依赖中国。”

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**Tracy Mumford**: And notably, the war in Iran has actually tied the US more closely to China. That's because China dominates global production of rare earth minerals, which are crucial components of US weapons from cruise missiles to fighter jets. One former US trade official told the times quote, "Every missile fired at Iran makes us that much more dependent in the near term on China."

</details>
