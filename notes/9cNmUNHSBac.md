### 转向在维持平衡中的作用

为什么在静止的自行车上很难保持平衡？

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Why is it hard to balance on a stationary bike?</p>
</details>

我想大多数人认为这是因为车轮没有转动，所以没有**陀螺效应**（gyroscopic effect: 旋转物体在惯性作用下保持其轴向不变的现象），但事实并非如此。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">I think most people believe it's because the wheels aren't spinning, so there's no gyroscopic effect, but that's not it.</p>

事实是，你通过转向来让自行车保持在你身下，但当你静止时，转向是无效的。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The truth is you use steering to keep the bike underneath you, but steering doesn't work when you're stationary.</p>
</details>

你的平衡并非主要来自于你身体在自行车上的位置，而是来自于你如何转向自行车以使其保持在你身下。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Your balance comes not so much from how you position your body over the bike, but by how you steer the bike to keep it underneath you.</p>
</details>

即使在直线行驶时，你也在不断进行微小的转向调整以保持平衡。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Even when going straight, you're constantly making small steering adjustments to maintain balance.</p>
</details>

你正在将前轮的接触点移动到你身下。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">You're moving the contact patch of the front wheel under you.</p>
</details>

你所做的，正是你在手上平衡扫帚柄时所做的事情。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">You're doing exactly what you do when you balance a broomstick on your hand.</p>
</details>
