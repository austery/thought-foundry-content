### 降息、资本与客户：经济增长的真正逻辑

人们普遍认为，当我们降低利率时，就能为企业提供更多低成本的资本，企业将这些资本投入运营，从而产生回报。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The belief is, when we cut rates, we provide more lower cost capital to companies, they put that capital to work, and it generates return.</p>
</details>

然而，每家公司的优劣、稳定性和实力，最终都取决于其客户基础。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Every company is only as good, or as stable, or as strong as its customer base.</p>
</details>

如果我们发现普通大众——我称之为**Joe Sixpack**（美国俚语：指典型的蓝领工人或普通消费者）——的客户群体正在苦苦挣扎，那么最终，所有（降息带来的）好处又将如何层层传导，并真正创造经济增长呢？

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And if we're seeing that the customer base of, let's call it the average Joe, and I call that Joe Sixpack, he's a buddy of mine, we all have a buddy, Joe Sixpack. And so if Joe Sixpack is struggling, then ultimately how is everything going to trickle down and create growth?</p>
</details>
