### AI在美国日益不受欢迎的现状

我最大的担忧之一是，**人工智能**（AI: Artificial Intelligence，模拟人类智能的计算机系统）在美国正变得非常不受欢迎。硅谷正在输掉这场围绕AI的舆论战。

<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">One of the major concerns I have is that AI is becoming deeply unpopular in America. Silicon Valley is losing the battle around AI.</p>
</details>

那些**悲观主义者**（Doomers: 指对未来持消极或末日论观点的人）现在正在用就业问题来恐吓民众。他们认为美国目前正在发生的所有裁员都是AI造成的。第二，他们看到电费上涨，也认为是AI导致的。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Doomers are now scaring people about jobs. They think all these job cuts that are going on in America are the result of AI. And number two, they're seeing their electric bills go up and they think that's also the result of AI.</p>
</details>

### 政治阻力与虚假叙事

我与许多共和党参议员和众议院议员交流过，他们表示害怕提及“AI”这个词，因为这会降低他们的支持率。我们必须扭转这种局面，因为这对美国来说是一个失败的策略。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">I've talked with a lot of Republican senators and House members who say they are afraid to mention the words AI because their popularity ratings go down. We need to get on the other side of that because that is a losing proposition for America.</p>
</details>

如果在美国形成一种趋势，即抵制AI在政治上很受欢迎，那么大卫，我认为你也会在州一级看到共和党州长们采取同样的立场。我认为这两种说法都是错误的叙事，但我们必须改变这种状况。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">If what takes hold here is that, you know, it's politically popular to push back against AI, then David, I think that's what you're seeing at the state levels with Republican governors as well. I think both of those are false narratives, but we need to get on the other side.</p>
</details>

### 对国家安全和经济安全的担忧

在中国，他们是不会放慢脚步的。因此，如果我们在这里踢了**乌龙球**（own goal: 比喻自毁前程或做出对自己不利的事情），因为我们误以为放慢发展是实现更大经济增长的途径，那么这将带来真正的麻烦，无论是对国家安全还是经济安全而言。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">In China, they're not going to slow down. So if we do an own goal here and slow down because we think somehow that this is the path to greater economic growth, it's going to be a real problem, both national security as well as economic security.</p>
</details>
