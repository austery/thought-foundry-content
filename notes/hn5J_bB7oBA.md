### OpenAI 研究部门的透明与协作文化

在目前拥有略少于600人的**OpenAI**（Open Artificial Intelligence: 一家美国人工智能研究实验室）研究部门中，每个人都了解一切。在研究领域，我们内部极其透明。整体文化非常协作，这是一个**正和博弈**（Positive Sum Game: 指所有参与者通过合作都能获得利益，而非零和博弈中一方得利必然以另一方损失为代价）的局面。OpenAI 的成功远非板上钉钉。大家普遍有一种共同的信念，即我们都需要相互依赖来完成工作，以实现这一共同的使命。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">In research at OpenAI, which is around slightly less than 600 people at the moment, everyone knows everything. We are extremely internally transparent when in research. The culture generally is very collaborative. It is a very positive sum game. The success of OpenAI is far from guaranteed. There is a lot of feeling of... shared faiths and the fact that we all need to rely on each other to do our job to achieve this shared mission.</p>
</details>

### 鼓励直接反馈以持续改进

确实会出现一些略显不必要的抱怨。但如果你不害怕指出这里不好或那里不好，那么你就离真相更近了一步，也更接近于识别出你真正应该改进和做得更好的地方。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">There truly will happen some complaints that are just a little bit unnecessary. But if you are not afraid to say that this is bad or that is bad, then you are a little bit closer to the truth and a little bit closer to identifying things that you actually should improve and should do better.</p>
</details>
