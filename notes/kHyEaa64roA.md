### 深度理解的本质

你如何判断自己是否对某事有深入的理解？理解**量子力学**（quantum mechanics: 描述原子和亚原子粒子行为的物理学理论）并非天赋异禀或某种奇迹般的能力。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">How do you know when you understand something deeply? There's no talent, a special miracle ability to understand quantum mechanics.</p>
</details>

**理查德·费曼**（Richard Feynman: 诺贝尔物理学奖得主，以其清晰的教学和解释能力闻名）是一位杰出的物理学家，他尤其擅长用简单的方式解释事物。原因在于他不断地自我提问。他对世界如何运作有着自己的模型，并反复地进行检验。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Richard Feynman is this brilliant physicist who also was very good at explaining things simply. And the reason is he constantly questioned himself. He had these models about how the world worked. and he tested them over and over and over.</p>
</details>

### 费曼技巧的核心理念

我刚上大学时，一位学长告诉我，判断我是否真正深入理解某事最好的方法就是将其教给别人。这正是以理查德·费曼命名的**费曼技巧**（Feynman Technique: 一种通过向他人教授来加深理解的学习方法）的核心宗旨。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">When I first got to college, there was an older student who taught me that the best way to know whether I really understood something deeply was to teach it to somebody else. And that is the core tenant of the Feynman technique that's named after Richard Feynman.</p>
</details>

### 费曼技巧的四个步骤

这项技巧包含四个步骤。首先是命名主题。你究竟想学习什么？

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">That technique has four steps. It starts by naming the topic. What is it that you're trying to learn about?</p>
</details>

第二步是，在此主题下写下你所知道的一切，进行全面的解释。例如，如果你想理解温度的概念，就写下你对温度的最佳定义，画出图表，展示分子的振动。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Now write a full explanation under that, that's step two, of everything you know about this topic. So if what you're trying to understand is the concept of temperature, write your best definition of temperature, draw diagrams, show the wiggling of molecules.</p>
</details>

完成这些后，第三步是尽可能清晰、简洁地解释这个概念。这样做会迫使你分解这个概念，而不仅仅是停留在其下所有词汇术语的层面。你要将其分解到如此简单的程度，以至于一个12岁的孩子也能在脑海中通过简单的图像理解你的解释。这也会揭示出你尚未完全理解这个概念的所有方面。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Once you've done all that, step three is to explain that concept as best as you can and as simply as you can. And what that does is it forces you to break down the concept, not just to the next. level of all of the vocab terms beneath it. You're breaking it down so far that even a 12 year old can with simple pictures in their mind understand what you're explaining. And that also reveals all the ways that you don't yet understand this concept.</p>
</details>

然后你可以进行第四步，即提出进一步的问题，进行研究，并完善你的模型。现在，你可以重新开始整个过程，再次尝试向他人解释这个概念。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And what you can do then is you can do step four, which is ask further questions and go do research and better your model. And now you can start the whole process all over again and you can try again to explain that concept to somebody else.</p>
</details>
