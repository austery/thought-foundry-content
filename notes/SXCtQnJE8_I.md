### 为什么选择深入使用 AI？
**Courtney**: 我在 Zapier 工作，这是一家自动化和 AI 编排公司。我非常热衷于使用 AI 来帮助自己摆脱那些无聊、重复和手动的工作，从而能够专注于更有意思的任务。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>
**Courtney**: So I work for Zapier, which is an automation and AI orchestration company. So of course it's part of our company ethos, but I am just personally super passionate about using AI because I think it can help work myself out of the boring, repetitive, manual parts of my role so I can do more interesting work.
</details>

### 自动化会议准备流程
**Courtney**: 我的每周会议准备代理是一个很好的例子。它会在周五早上八点自动运行，帮助我为下周的会议做准备。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>
**Courtney**: So this is essentially my weekly meeting prep agent which on Fridays I used to have maybe let's say 2 hours blocked 30 minutes to like do a retro on that week and anything I need to change but then like spending an hour or so really diving into the next week and what I need to be prepared for.
</details>
