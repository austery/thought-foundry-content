### 快休闲理念的诞生与消费者需求

我们真正发展出了一种理念，它就是今天所称的**快休闲**（Fast Casual: 介于快餐和正餐之间，提供更高品质食材和更好用餐环境的餐饮模式）。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">We really developed the ideology that became what is called today fast casual.</p>
</details>

这并非一个非常复杂的概念。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">It wasn't very complicated.</p>
</details>

在1992、1993年间，我们开始观察市场，发现每三到四名消费者中，就有一名在光顾**快餐**（Fast Food: 以快速服务和标准化产品为特点的餐饮模式）时是捏着鼻子进去的。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">We began to look around and could see, it's been '92, '93, that one out of three consumers, one out of four consumers held their noses when they went into fast food.</p>
</details>

于是我们不禁会问：“他们到底在寻找什么？”我知道我们花了一两年时间走访各地，倾听人们的心声。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And you said, "What is it that they're looking for?" I know we spent a year or two on the road listening to people.</p>
</details>

我们听到的是，许多消费者真正想要的是真实的食物、能吸引他们的用餐环境、由关心顾客的人提供的服务，以及一种能提升自我感受而非消耗自我的体验，而这正是他们在传统快餐中所感受到的。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And what you heard was that so many of these consumers, what they really thought was real food, environments that engage them, served by people that cared, and they actually wanted an experience that elevated their sense of self, not depleted it, which is what they experienced in fast food.</p>
</details>

于是我们开始思考，如果我们能创造出那样的环境，我们就能创造出真正能提升人们体验的事物。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And we began to say if we could create that kind of environment, we could create something that actually elevated people.</p>
</details>
