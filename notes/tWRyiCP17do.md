### 自由落体与物理极限

因为在最顶端有10米的**自由落体**（Freefall: 物体在重力作用下不受其他阻力影响的运动状态），所以你不会是滑行，而是直接坠落。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">At the top, there's a 10-meter freefall, so you wouldn't roll; you would plummet.</p>
</details>

事实上，即使是他出发的位置，也几乎达到了物理上的极限。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">In fact, even where he launched from was near the absolute limit of what's physically possible.</p>
</details>

### 高速震动：人类反应的瓶颈

因为当你滑行速度超过每小时30公里时，就会发生一些奇怪的事情。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Because as you start skating faster than 30 km/h, something strange happens.</p>
</details>

滑板会在你脚下剧烈晃动。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The board starts to shake violently underneath your feet.</p>
</details>

人类平衡的最快反应时间大约是100毫秒。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The fastest human reaction time to balance is about 100 milliseconds.</p>
</details>

随着速度的增加，滑板的震动会越来越快，所以当你的滑板向一个方向移动时，你可能还在试图弥补上一次的晃动。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">As the speed increases, the board's vibrations come in faster and faster. Your board might be moving one way while you're still trying to compensate for the previous wiggle.</p>

最终，你会让这些晃动变得越来越糟，直到情况变得非常糟糕，你直接从滑板上摔下来。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

</details>
