<details>

<details>

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">As evidence for this claim, Yi Fuxian provided the example that when China introduced the two-child policy in 2016, the National Health and Family Planning Commission announced 18.85 million births, an 827% jump from just the previous year, and some provinces like Shandong and Xinjiang reported increases of 56% and 75%, respectively.</p>
</details>

然而，至关重要的是，疫苗接种和入学数据显示没有相应的增长，这为我们提供了相当确凿的证据，表明这次“婴儿潮”很大程度上是为了让政策看起来成功而捏造的。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Crucially, however, vaccine and school enrollment data showed no corresponding increase, providing substantial evidence that this baby boom was largely fabricated to portray the policy as successful.</p>

尽管有证据表明中国夸大了出生人数，但联合国并未将其纳入考量，这加剧了联合国可能相信今天出生的人口远多于实际出生人数的问题。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Despite this evidence that China inflates its birth figures, the UN does not account for it, exacerbating the problem that the UN likely believes far more people are being born today than is actually the case.</p>
