### 开启内容3.0时代：AI驱动的互动体验

一幅经过巧妙处理的《蒙娜丽莎》画像，月球表面的TEDAI，以及一个你正在探索的3D奇幻世界——它们有什么共同之处？

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">An image of the Mona Lisa with a twist. TEDAI, on the surface of the Moon. A 3D fantasy world that you can see being explored here. What do they have in common?</p>
</details>

它们并非以传统意义上由人类创造，而是由人工智能（AI）生成。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">They weren't created by humans in the traditional sense. They were generated by AI.</p>
</details>

这正是我所称的**内容3.0**（Content 3.0: 由AI生成并支持用户互动的数字内容）的开端，它开启了我们前所未有的可能性。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">That is the very beginning of what I call content 3.0. It opens up possibilities we've never had before.</p>
</details>

它让故事讲述者、艺术家、创作者与我们观众之间能够共同创作内容，与他们的创作互动，与他们的角色对话，探索他们的世界，并且我们每个人都能沉浸在他们创作中最享受的部分。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Co-creating content together between storytellers, artists, creators, and us viewers. Interacting with their creations, talking with their characters, exploring their worlds and each and every one of us leaning into what we enjoy most in their creations.</p>
</details>

我预计这将对媒体和娱乐领域产生与从定时广播转向点播流媒体同样深远的影响。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">I expect this to have as profound an impact on the media and entertainment landscape as the shift from scheduled broadcasting to on-demand streaming.</p>
</details>

这是一场真正的范式转变，将使体验比以往任何时候都更具吸引力。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">A true paradigm shift that will make experiences more engaging than ever.</p>
</details>

让我们来深入探讨这可能意味着什么。为此，我们将使用数字内容的分类法。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Let's unpack what this could look like. To do so, we'll use a taxonomy of digital content.</p>
</details>

### 数字内容的演变：从1.0到3.0

**内容1.0**（Content 1.0: 传统专业制作、单向观看的数字内容）是仅供你观看的内容。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Content 1.0 is content that is just viewed by you.</p>
</details>

我今天用“观看”这个词来指代我们能体验到的所有类型的内容。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">I use the word “view” today for all types of content we can experience.</p>
</details>

**内容2.0**（Content 2.0: 用户生成、上传和分享的数字内容）可能是由你上传的。想想现代社交媒体。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Content 2.0 is possibly uploaded by you. Think about modern social media.</p>
</details>

**内容3.0**是由你生成的。专业人士可能已经训练了模型，准备了设置，但最终，你也是创作的一部分。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Content 3.0 is generated by you. Professionals may have trained the models, prepared the setting for it, but in the end, you are a part of creating it.</p>
</details>

让我们仔细看看。内容1.0是专业的。通常许多人聚在一起创造它，并为他们的受众提供最佳体验。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Let's have a closer look. Content 1.0 is professional. Often many people come together to create it and the best experiences for their audience.</p>
</details>

想想书籍、文章、音乐、电影、游戏等。它在互联网上创造了，并且仍在创造大量财富。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Think about books, articles, music, movies, games, etc. It made and makes a lot of money on the internet.</p>
</details>

制作成本也非常高。整个公司都围绕着它的创作、拥有分发渠道并旨在塑造流行文化而建立。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">It's also very expensive to make. Entire companies are built around creating it, owning their distribution channels, and aiming to shape pop culture.</p>
</details>

内容2.0是个人化的。从简单的个人主页、表情包和博客开始，随着消费级录制设备的普及和媒体共享平台变得更加强大，它演变为包含越来越高保真度的内容。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Content 2.0 is personal. What started with simple home pages for everyone, memes and blogs, evolved to include content with increasing fidelity as consumer recording devices became more prevalent and media-sharing platforms more powerful.</p>
</details>

这最终形成了现代社交媒体，数十亿人正在创作和分享内容。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">This culminates in modern social media. With billions of people creating and sharing content.</p>
</details>

我们预计仅在2025年，内容1.0和2.0之间将产生超过100**泽字节**（Zettabytes: 数据存储单位，1泽字节等于1万亿吉字节）的数据。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Between content 1.0 and 2.0, we expect to create over 100 zettabytes of data in 2025 alone.</p>
</details>

如果我们能及时设置一台高清摄像机，以每小时3吉字节（典型的**高清视频流**）的速度录制，这台摄像机将不得不从38亿年前开始录制才能记录下如此大量的数据。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">If we could set up an HD camera back in time, recording three gigabytes an hour, a typical HD video stream, this camera would have to start recording 3.8 billion years in the past to record this amount of data.</p>
</details>

最初不会有很多动态可看，因为那远远早于地球上复杂生命的出现。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">There would not be much motion to watch at first, since that is way before the dawn of complex life on Earth.</p>
</details>

如果这是一部20集的电视剧，恐龙的整个时期可能只有一集。而30万年的人类历史几乎不会在片尾字幕中出现。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

如果我们幸运的话，它可能只是下一件大事的预告。这就是内容量之大令人难以置信的程度。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

</details>

而这仅仅是我们预计在2025年一年内增加的数据量。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And that is just what we're expected to add in 2025 alone.</p>
</details>

### AI模型的力量与沉浸式世界的构建

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Now, this great amount of data and content is what powers the dawn of AI models and overall content 3.0. It is generative.</p>
</details>

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">With these amounts of data and content, AI models can learn to understand it, even to reproduce it and produce it.</p>
</details>

例如，**大型语言模型**（Large Language Models: 经过海量文本数据训练，能够理解和生成人类语言的AI模型）如今已无需介绍。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">For instance, large language models need no introduction today.</p>
</details>

**GPT-3**（Generative Pre-trained Transformer 3: 由OpenAI开发的大型语言模型）在数千亿个词汇上进行了训练，这些词汇来自文章、书籍、社交媒体帖子和代码。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

### 互动叙事与个性化体验的未来

当然，我们可以将内容3.0模型作为工具来创造更多内容1.0和2.0。

<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Of course, we can use content 3.0 models as tools to create more of content 1.0 and 2.0.</p>
</details>

想象一下一个流媒体服务，你描述你梦想中的电影，它在几分钟内就会出现。一个社交平台，你上传一个视频，只需一个提示，它就会立即转化为专业的网红剪辑。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Just imagine a streaming service where you describe your dream movie and it appears in minutes. A social platform where you upload a video and it's just a prompt, and it is immediately transformed into a professional influencer clip.</p>
</details>

所以所有这些都可能成为现实，但我认为这仍然没有充分发挥这些模型的潜力。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">So all of this may become a reality, but I think it's still not realizing the full potential of these models.</p>
</details>

这就是我开始梦想新型叙事、互动世界和体验的地方，梦想着我们能以比对话框更自然的方式与角色互动，梦想着我们能探索没有人工边界的世界，梦想着非预设的叙事。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And this is where I start to dream about new kinds of storytelling, interactive worlds and experiences, about characters that we can interact with in so much more natural ways than just dialogue boxes, about worlds that we can explore with no artificial boundaries, about narratives that are not predetermined.</p>
</details>

自从青少年时期玩3D游戏，开发模组、关卡、角色以来，我的旅程一直朝着这个方向发展，我认为现在我们有机会借助内容3.0实现巨大的飞跃。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Since being a teenager and playing 3D games, developing mods, levels, characters, my journey has taken me further in this direction and I think now we have an opportunity to take a huge leap forward thanks to content 3.0.</p>

原因是，我们现在可以以等于或高于消费速度的速度创造有趣的内容。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The reason is that we can now create interesting content at or above the rate of consumption.</p>
</details>

这个事实的影响怎么强调都不为过。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The impact of this fact cannot be overstated.</p>
</details>

<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

在人类历史的任何时刻，我们都从未能够做到这一点。即兴表演之所以如此有趣，是因为演员们在舞台上没有计划地表演，即兴创作接下来发生的事情。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

<details>

</details>

或者观众主动联系他们，告诉他们反派在哪里，或者提供一个情节转折的新想法。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

</details>

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Or strategize with him about where the villain can be found, but you see the point.</p>
</details>

它开启了一个全新的、迄今为止未被充分探索的媒介。

<details>

### 内容生产模式的经济学变革

那么，让我们来看看。对于内容1.0，制作人齐心协力创造内容。

<details>
