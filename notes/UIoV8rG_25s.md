### SpaceX 深度透视：估值与发射频次的逻辑

**[Thomas Laffont]**: 我知道很多人会讨论 **SpaceX**。所以我想分享一下我们作为投资者是如何看待 SpaceX 的。这样当你考虑是否要持有一股，或者只是想在酒会上显得很聪明时，可以从我们的知识中获益。

当我们研究 SpaceX 时，发现与其估值相关的头号驱动因素是**发射频次**，这在直觉上是合理的。如果你的业务是发射，你发射得越多，你的价值就越高。数据证明了这一点。

但我想向你们展示另一个根本不同的比例：如果我们将估值除以发射次数会怎样？你可以看到，这个比例曾在一个固定区间内波动了一段时间，然后开始真正攀升。

市场是理性的。那么为什么市场在 SpaceX 发射次数更多时，给它的单次发射估值反而比创业初期更高呢？

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**[Thomas Laffont]**: So I know SpaceX, a lot of people are going to talk about SpaceX. So I thought I would share a little bit of how we as investors think about SpaceX. So that as you think about whether it's a stock that you want to own or you just want to seem smart at a cocktail party, you can benefit from our knowledge. The first thing that pops out when we look and study SpaceX is that the number one driver correlated to the valuation of SpaceX is cadence of launches, which intuitively makes sense. If your business is the launch business, the more you launch, the higher your value should be. So I think we see that in the data. But there's another fundamentally different ratio that I want to point you to which is what if we took the valuation, we divided it by the number of launches. What would that look like? Well, you can see it was kind of in a fixed range for a while and then it really started to move up. And we believe that markets are rational. And so we started thinking, well, why is it that the market is valuing SpaceX higher on a perlaunch basis when it's launching more than when it was just starting out?

</details>

**[Thomas Laffont]**: 我的基本观点（我们称之为 **CODE 框架**）是：原因是 **SpaceX 的业务模型质量随着发射次数的增加而提高**。

在第一阶段（**前星链时代**），你只是在试射火箭。我们知道火箭很难，可能只有几个政府客户，这是一种一次性收入业务，且不可预测。

接着进入**初始坡度**阶段，现在你可能拥有了一个**星链 (Constellation)**。为什么星链很重要？因为它是一个终端市场，是一项**经常性收入** (Recurring Revenue) 业务。你发射的卫星越多，你的订户就越多，收入也就越多。

现在你可以从坡度进入**规模化**阶段。现在你不止有一个星链，你有多个星链。我们相信各种各样的公司、政府和军队都想拥有自己的星链来控制自己的命运。

最终，你进入了**平台**化阶段，我们知道在技术时代这些平台有多么昂贵。平台意味着你不仅在核心业务中拥有更多客户，还拥有了新业务，可能是空间数据中心，可能是去月球或火星的可选性，以及其他太空应用。

<details>
<summary>Original English</summary>

**[Thomas Laffont]**: And my fundamental view, and we'll kind of call this our code framework, is that the reason is that the quality of SpaceX's business model increases the more you launch. So, in phase one, which we call pre-constellation, you're just trying your rockets. And we know rockets are hard and maybe you have a few government customers and that's a one-time revenue business and it's unpredictable. Then you get into your initial ramp and now you might have one constellation. So, why is a constellation important? Well, it's an end market and it's a recurring revenue business. The more satellites you put up, the more subscribers you have, the more revenue, etc. Now you can move from ramp into scale. Now you don't just have one constellation, you have multiple constellations. And ultimately, we believe that a ride a wide variety of companies and governments and militaries will want to own their own constellation so they can control their own destiny. So now you move into being a scale business which ultimately becomes a platform and we know how valuable these platforms are in this technology age and platform means not only do you have many more customers in your core business but you also have new businesses. It could be space data centers. It could be the optionality of the moon and Mars and other space applications.

</details>

### 幂律法则：为什么大公司更容易实现 10 倍增长？

**[Thomas Laffont]**: 这个时代的一个定义性特征就是这些公司扩张的速度。看看 **Anthropic**，它的规模扩张速度是我们在 **PC**、互联网或移动时代从未见过的。

这里有一个有趣的分析，我很想和 Besties 讨论一下。我们查看了三类公司，并询问：在每一类中，你获得 **10 倍 (10x)** 回报的可能性是多少？对于我们这种成长型投资者来说，10 倍已经非常出色了，很难找到。

数据显示，如果你是一家**独角兽** ($1B+)，你有一天成为**十角兽** ($10B+) 的概率约为 **8%**。

如果你已经是一家十角兽，那你成为**千亿美金公司** ($100B+) 的概率并没有好多少，只有 **8% 到 13%**。

但有趣的是，如果你已经是一家 **Centacorn**（千亿美金级别及以上），你拥有 10 倍增长记录的概率竟然高达 **31%**。这在我的观点中与大家预期的非常不同。

### 营收之辩：AI 的真实变现路径
