<p class="english-text">This sounds like a crazy fucking thing to say on a podcast in 2025. How do we start a conversation with another human being?</p>
</details>

<details>

<p class="english-text">I've got a favorite technique that I use. And it requires courage.</p>

### Hi-Lo Buffalo：连接人心的游戏

这是一个简单的游戏，叫做**“Hi-Lo Buffalo”**（高光-低谷-有趣特质）。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">And it's a game, simple game, called "Hi-Lo Buffalo".</p>
</details>

“Hi”代表你生活中进展顺利的事情，即**高光时刻**。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">"Hi" is something that's going great for you.</p>
</details>

“Lo”代表你生活中不太顺利的事情，即**低谷经历**。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">"Lo" is something that's not so great for you.</p>
</details>

“Buffalo”则代表你身上一些有趣或特别的特质，即**有趣特质**。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">"Buffalo" is something interesting about you.</p>
</details>

你会直接告诉他们我们要玩“Hi-Lo Buffalo”这个游戏吗？我会在我想要建立连接的初次见面的人身上使用这个游戏。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Do you say it to them that we're going to do "Hi-Lo Buffalo"? I play this game with people I meet that I want to connect with.</p>
</details>

假设你已经和苏珊共事了12个月。你可以和苏珊坐下来，然后说：“苏珊，我知道这听起来有点疯狂。我在《CEO日记》上听一个叫Vin的人说过，如果你想与某人建立连接，并且你重视这种连接，那就玩一个叫做‘Hi-Lo Buffalo’的游戏。你愿意玩吗？开始吧！”

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Say you've been working with Susan for 12 months. Just sit down with Susan and go, "Susan, I know this is a bit crazy. Listen to a guy name. Vin on Dara over CEO, he said, "If you want to connect with someone and you care about the connection, play a game called 'Hilo Buffalo'. Are you game? Play."</p>
</details>

我提供了三个机会来激发对话，但实际上这里有六条线索，因为我有三条，你也有三条。你刚刚给了我三件事，我也有三条线索可以选择。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">I'm giving three opportunities for a conversation to spark, but in actuality there are six threads here because there's three from me, there's three from you. You just gave me three things. I've got three threads to choose from.</p>
</details>
