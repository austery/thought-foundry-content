### 自动化实验报告的演示

我将向您展示这项技术在实际中的应用。让我们在一个实验结果上运行它。我只是简单地指示它撰写实验结果，并提供了实验名称：“垂直产品瓷砖图片”。它立即开始工作，找到了结果，并识别出相关规则。现在它将为我处理所有这些信息。它已经找到了我们需要的一切，并开始撰写文档，这令人欣喜。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">I'm going to show you what this thing looks like in reality. So let's just run it on an experiment result. So I've just said, please write up the experiment results for and I've given it the name of the experiment, which is vertical product tile images. And straight off, it's gone off and it's found its results. Great. It's found the rules. And now it's going to start working this all out for me. It has found everything we need. It's starting to write out the doc, which is nice to see.</p>
</details>

我现在将把它排队转换为一个**Notion**（项目管理和笔记工具）文档。这样，在我阅读实际结果的同时，它就会开始撰写Notion文档。让我们来看看这些要点。我们不仅获得了更多的零售商，而且我们现有的零售商质量也更高。因此，这作为第一步看起来非常不错。这看起来很棒。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">I'm just going to go ahead and queue up, turn this into a notion. So as soon as I've read it, while we look at the actual results, it will start writing the notion doc and let's have a look at these takeaways. So it's not only have we got more retailers, they're actually higher quality retailers, the ones we've got. So this looked good as a first pass. This looks. great.</p>
</details>

### 自动化带来的效率提升

如您所见，我刚刚请求了Notion链接，瞧，我立刻就得到了一个精美的文档，可以与所有人分享，其中包含正确的颜色编码、关键要点。甚至还有一个小惊喜——虽然它在处理小开关（toggle）时总有些麻烦，但在文档底部，我还让它生成了一个更精简的**Slack**（团队协作和通讯平台）消息。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">So as you can see, I've just asked for the Notion link and look straight away, I've got a nice document I can share around with everyone with all the right color codes, the takeaways and even as a little bonus, let's see if that always has trouble getting things in a little toggle, but right in the bottom here, I've even asked it to spit out a slack with an even more summarized version.</p>
</details>

这样我就可以直接将它发布到正确的查看频道，并立即获得批准。公司里的任何人都可以开始这样做，这意味着我们可以将越来越多的此类任务下放给工程师、**PMs**（产品经理: Product Managers）和其他人来撰写这些内容并进行分析。这又可以极大地加速我们的**发布速度**（launch velocity: 指产品或功能推向市场的速度），我们对此感到非常兴奋。

Anyone in the business can start doing this, which means we can pass more and more of these things. down to engineers, PMs, other people to write this kind of stuff and do the analysis for them, which again can just massively accelerate our launch velocity affair, which we're really excited for.</p>
