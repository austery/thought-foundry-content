### 驾驭颠覆性变革：领导者的必备技能

我无法想象有任何一种情况，我会不建议一位领导者至少去关注市场中正在发挥作用的**颠覆性力量**（Disruptive Forces: 指对现有市场、行业或商业模式造成根本性改变的力量），并尝试应对其市场内外正在发生的某些颠覆。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">I can't think of a single circumstance where I would not tell a leader to at least be watching for the disruptive forces at work in the market and experimenting with some of the disruptions that are taking place in and around their market.</p>
</details>

存在一套可重复的技能和行为，任何人都可以遵循，任何人都可以通过有意识的练习来提高，从而增加他们发现、塑造和响应颠覆性变革的可能性。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">There are a set of repeatable skills, repeatable behaviors that anybody can follow, that anybody can get better at with conscious practice, which can increase their odds of spotting, shaping, and responding to disruptive change.</p>
</details>

这是一项非常容易学习的技能，今天的领导者没有任何借口说“哦，我就是没有能力做这个”。你有这个能力。发展这些技能与否，是一个选择。

<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">It is a very learnable skill, and there's no leader today that has an excuse of, 'Oh, I just don't have it in me to do this.' You do. It's a choice to develop those skills or not.</p>
</details>
