### 打造最佳竞争替代品是商业成功的关键

我所信奉的一切商业理念都始于三个词：**最佳竞争替代品**（Better Competitive Alternative: 指在市场中提供比现有产品或服务更优越的选择）。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">Everything I believe in about business starts with three words: better competitive alternative.</p>
</details>

这个世界并不需要再多一家企业。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The world doesn't need another business.</p>
</details>

它当然也不需要再多一家餐馆。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">It certainly doesn't need another restaurant.</p>
</details>

我们所做一切的全部目标，就是为某个目标客户打造出他们能找到的**最佳替代品**。我这样说——听起来可能像花哨的**MBA**（Master of Business Administration: 工商管理硕士，常指专业商业管理知识）术语——但事实并非如此。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">The whole objective of everything we do is to build something that, for some target customer, is the best alternative they can find. And what I mean by that—it sounds like fancy MBA talk—it isn't.</p>
</details>

它的真正含义是，你的目标消费者会绕过所有竞争对手——所有那些提供类似产品或服务的人——并选择来到你这里，因为你做得比任何人都好。如果你能做到这一点，你就能获胜。
<details>
<summary>View/Hide Original English</summary>
<p class="english-text">All it means is that your target consumer is going to walk past all of your competitors—all the people doing something similar to you—and choose to come to you because you do it better than anybody else. If you're able to do that, you can win.</p>
</details>
